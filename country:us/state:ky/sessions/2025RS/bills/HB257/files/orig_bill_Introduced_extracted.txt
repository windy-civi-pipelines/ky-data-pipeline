UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 1069
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 431 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section:
6 (a) "Eligible conviction" means any criminal conviction meeting the
7 requirements of KRS 431.073(1); and
8 (b) "Expungeable conviction" means any eligible conviction if:
9 1. Five (5) years have passed since the completion of any term of
10 incarceration and any nonmonetary conditions of release; and
11 2. The person has not in the previous five (5) years been convicted of a
12 felony or misdemeanor.
13 (2) On August 1, 2025, and on the first day of each month thereafter, the Department
14 of Kentucky State Police and the Administrative Office of the Courts shall
15 identify eligible convictions that have not been expunged.
16 (3) On August 15, 2025, and on the fifteenth day of each month thereafter, the
17 Administrative Office of the Courts shall produce a list of eligible convictions that
18 are expungeable convictions, and shall communicate each expungeable
19 conviction to the court in which the conviction occurred.
20 (4) (a) Within thirty (30) days of receipt of a notice of an expungeable conviction,
21 the court shall order the judgment vacated, dismiss with prejudice any
22 charges that are eligible for expungement under this section, and order
23 expunged all records in the custody of the court and any records in the
24 custody of any other agency or official, including law enforcement records.
25 (b) The order expunging the records shall not require any action by the person
26 with the expungable conviction.
27 (c) Upon entry of an order expunging a conviction pursuant to this section, the
