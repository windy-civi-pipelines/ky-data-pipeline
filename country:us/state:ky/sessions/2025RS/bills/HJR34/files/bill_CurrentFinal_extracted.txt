UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS HJR 34/GA
1 A JOINT RESOLUTION relating to contingent appropriations.
2 WHEREAS, it is the responsibility of the General Assembly to monitor the
3 spending of state funds for the good of the Commonwealth; and
4 WHEREAS, with that responsibility in mind, certain appropriations were
5 contingent upon specific duties being fulfilled by the Kentucky Community and
6 Technical College System, in collaboration with the Council on Postsecondary
7 Education; and
8 WHEREAS, the General Assembly recognizes the need to improve and advance the
9 existing Kentucky Community and Technical College System; and
10 WHEREAS, 2024 Ky. Acts ch. 175, Part I, J., 11., (8) requires the Kentucky
11 Community and Technical College System, in collaboration with the Council on
12 Postsecondary Education, to submit a proposal for approval by the General Assembly
13 that analyzes certain recommendations; and
14 WHEREAS, that proposal has been submitted;
15 NOW, THEREFORE,
16 Be it resolved by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
17 Section 1. The General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky hereby
18 approves and authorizes the Office of State Budget Director to release capital
19 construction funds to improve and advance the existing Kentucky Community and
20 Technical College System, in collaboration with the Council on Postsecondary
21 Education, in accordance with 2024 Ky. Acts ch. 175, Part I, J., 11., (8).
