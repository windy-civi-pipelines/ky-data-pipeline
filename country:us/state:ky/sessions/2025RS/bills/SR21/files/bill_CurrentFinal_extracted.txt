3 WHEREAS, Barry Bertram entered this world on August 29, 1935, in Taylor

16 a Commonwealth's Attorney, always seeking a just and fair outcome for all parties
17 involved; and
18 WHEREAS, as a captivating and humorous storyteller, Barry Bertram enjoyed
19 sharing his life experiences and imparted many words of wisdom to his family, friends,
20 and colleagues; and
