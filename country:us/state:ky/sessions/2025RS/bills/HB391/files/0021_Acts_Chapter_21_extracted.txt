Section 1:
CHAPTER 21 1
CHAPTER 21
( HB 391 )
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
Section 1. KRS 217.187 is amended to read as follows:
If a person sells less than five hundred (500)[one hundred fifty (150)] gallons of pure and unadulterated honey in a
year that he or she produced in Kentucky[ off the farm], the person shall not be required to process the honey in a
certified honey house or food processing establishment, nor shall the person be required to obtain a permit from the
cabinet. The person shall be required to comply with the other provisions of KRS 217.005 to 217.215 as those
provisions apply.
Signed by Governor March 15, 2025.
