UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 1611
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 6.935 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) The Attorney General, or an assistant attorney general designated by him or her,
5 the Auditor of Public Accounts, cabinet secretaries, and the heads of other state
6 agencies shall assist the Legislative Oversight and Investigations Committee in
7 whatever manner the co-chairs deem that these officials can be helpful.
8 (2) Staffing and support services shall be provided to the Legislative Oversight and
9 Investigations Committee pursuant to KRS 7.090.
