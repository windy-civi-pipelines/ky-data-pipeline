3 WHEREAS, Dr. John Henderson Scott, III, was born at Pikeville Medical Center

5 WHEREAS, Dr. Scott received his Bachelor’s Degree from Eastern Kentucky

7 WHEREAS, Dr. Scott practiced medicine for more than 30 years and was one of

9 WHEREAS, a cattle and horse enthusiast, Dr. Scott bred and owned Thoroughbreds

12 WHEREAS, Dr. Scott leaves behind two beloved sons of whom he was

17 great-niece, Cora Bea Scott; and numerous other family members and friends;
18 NOW, THEREFORE,
19 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
20 Kentucky:
21 Section 1. The Senate hereby expresses its most profound sympathy upon the
22 passing of Dr. John Henderson Scott, III.
23 Section 2. When the Senate adjourns this day, it does so in honor and loving
24 memory of Dr. John Henderson Scott, III.
25 Section 3. The Clerk of the Senate is directed to transmit a copy of this
26 Resolution to Senator Phillip Wheeler for delivery to the family.
