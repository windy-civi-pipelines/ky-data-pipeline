7 SECTION 6. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 311 IS CREATED TO
8 READ AS FOLLOWS:
9 (1) A collaboration agreement shall include the:
10 (a) Physician assistant's scope of practice as determined at the practice setting
11 and as set forth in this chapter;
12 (b) Communication and decision-making process for the physician assistant,
13 the collaborating physician, and other members of the health care team;
14 and
15 (c) Signatures of the physician assistant and the collaborating physician.
16 (2) A copy of the signed collaboration agreement must be kept on file at the
17 physician assistant's practice location and made available to the board upon
18 request.
19 (3) The level of collaboration required under this section shall be determined at the
20 practice setting, including a collaborating physician, physician group practice,
21 private practice, or by the credentialing and privileging system of a health care
22 facility.
23 (4) A collaborating physician shall not enter into a collaboration agreement with
24 more than four (4) physician assistants.
25 (5) The Kentucky Board of Medical Licensure shall be notified within five (5)
26 business days of the dissolution of a collaboration agreement by a physician
27 assistant.
