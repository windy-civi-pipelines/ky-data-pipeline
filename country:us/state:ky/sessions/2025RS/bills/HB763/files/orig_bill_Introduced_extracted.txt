Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 320
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 502.010 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) A person is guilty of an offense committed by an innocent or irresponsible person
5 when he or she:
6 (a) Acts with the culpability that is sufficient for commission of that offense; and
7 (b) Causes that innocent or irresponsible person to engage in conduct constituting
8 the offense.
9 (2) As used in this section, an "innocent or irresponsible person" includes anyone who
10 is not guilty of the offense in question, despite his or her participation, because of:
11 (a) Criminal irresponsibility or other legal incapacity or exemption; or
12 (b) Unawareness of the criminal nature of the conduct in question or the
13 defendant's criminal purpose; or
14 (c) Any other factor precluding the mental state sufficient for the commission of
15 the offense in question.
