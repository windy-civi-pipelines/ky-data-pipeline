Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 886
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 198B IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) The department shall recognize and allow on-the-job training equivalencies of
6 internship and cooperative placement hours to count toward the hours necessary
7 to obtain licensure in a profession regulated by the department, including but not
8 limited to electrician, plumbing, and heating, ventilation, and air conditioning
9 licenses.
10 (2) The maximum equivalencies to count toward licensure under subsection (1) of
11 this section shall be:
12 (a) For electrician licenses, up to four thousand (4,000) hours;
13 (b) For plumber licenses, up to two (2) years; and
14 (c) For heating, ventilation, and air conditioning licenses, up to three thousand
15 (3,000) hours.
16 (3) The department shall establish a reporting system under this section through the
17 promulgation of administrative regulations in accordance with KRS Chapter
18 13A.
