Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 961
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 244 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) A retail drink license under KRS Chapters 241 to 244 shall not be issued or
6 renewed without proof that the applicant or licensee is covered under a liquor
7 liability insurance policy in an amount not less than:
8 (a) Two hundred fifty thousand dollars ($250,000) for the bodily injury or
9 death of one (1) person in any one (1) incident arising out of the sale or
10 service of alcoholic beverages on the licensed premises; and
11 (b) Five hundred thousand dollars ($500,000) for the bodily injury or death of
12 more than one (1) person in any one (1) incident arising out of the sale or
13 service of alcoholic beverages on the licensed premises.
14 (2) Proof of the insurance coverage required by this section shall be made to the
15 department in a form acceptable to the department.
