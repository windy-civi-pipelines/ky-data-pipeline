2 D1 Nation 10U Youth Football national champions.
3 WHEREAS, on December 16, 2024, at the Harold Patterson Sports Center in
4 Dallas, Texas, the Tenth Street Cardinals from Louisville, Kentucky, defeated Buckeyes
5 United of Mississippi by a score of 27 to 0 to win the 2024 D1 Nation 10U Youth
6 Football national championship; and
7 WHEREAS, the win in the championship game capped a season that saw the
8 Cardinals compile a record of 22 wins against 5 losses, traveling to Ohio, Indiana,
9 Tennessee, and Georgia before their appearance at the championship tournament in
10 Texas; and
11 WHEREAS, in the championship tournament, the Cardinals outscored their
12 opponents by a cumulative score 115 to 20; and
13 WHEREAS, members of the Cardinals included: Messiah Ezell, Maximus Evans,
14 Avery Weatherford, Xavier Thompson, Thomas Scott, Adrian Cook, Davon Cook, Tylen
15 White, Willie James, Jayden Brown, Prince Sublett, Bryson Harris, Maleek Brent,
16 Ahmoni Dale, Kevontis Jordan, Kamron Reed, Stephan Adams, Micah Dandy, Roman
17 Shoulders, Jahmell Graves, Da'vion Bonhom, Kyus Jarmon, Adarreis Rucker, Justin
18 Pace, Ke’Aire Moore, Kendall Lampkins, Quavion King, Nahsir Legrande, Dazman
19 Johnson Jr., Jayden Brown, Dazman Croom Jr., and Stephen Bowmen; and
20 WHEREAS, the Cardinals were led by head coach Thomas Jackson III, along with
21 assistant coaches Robert Gatewood, Allen Evans III, Robert Shoulders, and Kevontis
22 Jordan Sr.; and
23 WHEREAS, the Tenth Street Cardinals organization has been around for over 30
24 years, using football as a catalyst to provide quality leadership and discipline to inner-city
25 youth; and
26 WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that these outstanding young men and their
27 coaches be recognized for their incredible achievements;
