3 WHEREAS, there are 22 hospitals in Kentucky with a SANE-ready designation,

5 WHEREAS, the Sexual Assault Response Team Advisory Committee identifies
6 increasing access to SANE care across the Commonwealth as one component of
7 addressing sexual violence that needs urgent action; and
8 WHEREAS, analysis of access to SANEs in the Commonwealth is necessary to

10 for citizens of the Commonwealth who are victims of sexual assault and abuse and
11 greater safety may be secured for all citizens of the Commonwealth;
12 NOW, THEREFORE,
13 Be it resolved by the House of Representatives of the General Assembly of the
14 Commonwealth of Kentucky, the Senate concurring therein:
15 Section 1. The staff of the Legislative Research Commission is directed to
16 complete a study of the access to Sexual Assault Nurse Examiners (SANE) in emergency
17 care settings. Staff shall consult with the members of the Sexual Assault Response Team
18 Advisory Committee established in KRS 403.707 in conducting the study.
19 Section 2. The study required by Section 1 of this Resolution shall include, to
20 the extent the data is available, but not be limited to an analysis of:

25 in emergency care settings and by which hospitals;
