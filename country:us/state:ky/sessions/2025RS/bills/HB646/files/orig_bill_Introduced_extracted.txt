Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 1832
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 216 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section, "medical product" means a product used to diagnose or
6 manage a disease, including a medical device, drug, or biologic.
7 (2) Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, a public or
8 government entity or any agency or division thereof shall not require an
9 individual to take, receive, or disclose whether the individual has taken or
10 received a medical product unless the manufacturer of the medical product is
11 liable for any death or serious injury caused by the medical product.
