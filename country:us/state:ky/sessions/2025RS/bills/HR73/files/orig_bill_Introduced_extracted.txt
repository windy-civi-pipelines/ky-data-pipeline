1 A RESOLUTION commending Taiwan for its relationship with the United States
2 and the Commonwealth of Kentucky.
3 WHEREAS, Taiwan and the United States of America are long-standing allies who
4 both deeply cherish the common values of democracy, peace, and prosperity, toward
5 which Taiwan continues to pursue integrated diplomacy and serves the best interests of
6 the Indo-Pacific region by deepening ties with other like-minded nations, safeguarding
7 regional security, and utilizing Taiwan’s technological and economic success to promote
8 partnerships on mutual benefits; and
9 WHEREAS, China repeatedly exerts economic coercion and stages military drills
10 directed toward Taiwan; and
11 WHEREAS, Taiwan is the United States’ eighth-largest trading partner, and the
12 United States is Taiwan’s second-largest trading partner, with an estimated $128 billion
13 worth of trade, and Taiwan is Kentucky’s 14th largest import and 20th largest export
14 market in 2023; and
15 WHEREAS, the United States' and Taiwan’s first agreement entry into force under
16 the US-Taiwan Initiative on 21st-Century Trade includes commitments on anti-
17 corruption, good regulatory practices, customs administration, trade facilitation, and
18 small and medium-sized enterprises; and
19 WHEREAS, Kentucky signed a collaboration agreement with Taiwan in 2023,
20 agreeing to collaborate in good faith to facilitate two-way trade and investment expansion
21 and industrial cooperation, another milestone following a bilateral education cooperation
22 memorandum of understanding signed in 2021;
23 NOW, THEREFORE,
24 Be it resolved by the House of Representatives of the General Assembly of the
25 Commonwealth of Kentucky:
26 Section 1. The House of Representatives supports constant talks as Taiwanese
27 companies are looking for a diverse and free market investment in the United States,
