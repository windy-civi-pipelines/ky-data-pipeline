1 NOW, THEREFORE,
2 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
3 Kentucky:
4 Section 1. The Senate urges the Cabinet for Health and Family Services to
5 explore and implement strategies to maximize the utilization of the SNAP E&T program.
6 Section 2. The Senate encourages the Cabinet for Health and Family Services to
7 engage with community-based organizations, employers, and educational institutions to
8 build partnerships that support the delivery of SNAP E&T services.
9 Section 3. The Senate urges the Cabinet for Health and Family Services identify
10 and address any barriers to participation in SNAP E&T and develop targeted outreach
11 initiatives to ensure that eligible individuals are aware of and can access these vital
12 services.
13 Section 4. The Senate encourages the Cabinet for Health and Family Services to
14 compile a report detailing findings, strategies, and recommendations for expanding
15 SNAP E&T utilization in Kentucky and to submit the report to the General Assembly by
16 December 1, 2025.
17 Section 5. The Senate recognizes that the benefits of expanding SNAP E&T
18 utilization include increased employment opportunities, economic self-sufficiency,
19 reduced poverty rates, and a strengthened workforce, all of which contribute to a brighter
20 and more prosperous future for Kentucky.
21 Section 6. The Clerk of the Senate is directed to transmit a copy of this
22 Resolution to the Secretary of the Cabinet for Health and Family Services, 275 East Main
23 Street, Frankfort, Kentucky 40621.
