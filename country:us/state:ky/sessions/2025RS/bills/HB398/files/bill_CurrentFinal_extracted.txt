2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 338.062 is amended to read as follows:
4 [After July 1, 2021, neither ]The Kentucky Occupational Safety and Health Standards
5 board,[ nor] the secretary, the secretary's designee, the commissioner, and the
6 commissioner's authorized representative shall not adopt, [or ]promulgate, or enforce
7 any occupational safety and health administrative regulation that the Occupational Safety
8 and Health Administration or the United States Department of Labor has not
9 promulgated, or that is more stringent than the corresponding federal provision enforced
10 by the United States Department of Labor under the Occupational Safety and Health Act
11 of 1970. Whereas the Occupational Safety and Health Act of 1970 does not apply to
12 public employees, the cabinet shall retain the authority to promulgate and enforce, as
13 necessary, administrative regulations pertaining to public employees.
14 Section 2. KRS 338.091 is amended to read as follows:
15 (1) Any party adversely affected or aggrieved by a final order of the review
16 commission may appeal within thirty (30) days to the Franklin Circuit Court on the
17 record for a review of such order. No new evidence may be introduced in the
18 Circuit Court. An appeal may be taken to the Court of Appeals from any decision of
19 the Circuit Court under this section.
20 (2) On appeal, the Franklin Circuit Court may award actual expenses incurred,
21 including court costs and attorney's fees, against the department.
22 (3) The department shall promulgate administrative regulations in accordance with
23 KRS Chapter 13A to implement this section. The administrative regulations shall
24 comply with the Equal Access to Justice Act, 28 U.S.C. sec. 2412.
25 (4) The commencement of a proceeding[proceedings] under this section shall [not,
26 unless ordered by the court, ]operate as a stay of an order of the review
27 commission.
