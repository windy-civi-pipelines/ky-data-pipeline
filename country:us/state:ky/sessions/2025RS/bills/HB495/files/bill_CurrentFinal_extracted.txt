Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS HB 495/VO
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 205 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary and unless required under
6 federal law, the Department for Medicaid Services and any managed care organization
7 with whom the department contracts for the delivery of Medicaid services are hereby
8 prohibited from expending any Medicaid funds on any of the following:
9 (1) Cross-sex hormones in amounts greater than would normally be produced
10 endogenously in a healthy person of the same age and sex; and
11 (2) Gender reassignment surgery to alter or remove physical or anatomical
12 characteristics or features that are typical for and characteristic of a person's
13 biological sex.
14 Section 2. Notwithstanding any law, administrative regulation, executive order,
15 or directive to the contrary, Executive Order 2024-632 shall be of no force or effect as of
16 the effective date of this Act.
17 Section 3. Notwithstanding any law, administrative regulation, executive order,
18 or directive to the contrary, any administrative regulation, executive order, or directive
19 that is identical to or substantially the same as Executive Order 2024-632 shall be of no
20 force or effect as of the effective date of this Act through January 1, 2028.
21 Section 4. Whereas the General Assembly seeks to protect the rights of all
22 Kentuckians, an emergency is declared to exist, and Sections 2 and 3 of this Act take
23 effect upon its passage and approval by the Governor or upon its otherwise becoming a
24 law.
