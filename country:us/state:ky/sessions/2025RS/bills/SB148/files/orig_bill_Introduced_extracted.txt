UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 1731
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 504.120 is amended to read as follows:
4 In cases in which the defendant provides evidence at trial of his or her mental illness or
5 insanity at the time of the offense, the jury or court may find the defendant:
6 (1) Guilty;
7 (2) Not guilty;
8 (3) Not guilty by reason of insanity at the time of the offense; or
9 (4) Guilty but mentally ill at the time of the offense.
