2 Commonwealth.
3 WHEREAS, the members of the General Assembly acknowledge the valuable role
4 and substantial impact Bosnian immigrants have in the Commonwealth; and
5 WHEREAS, the massacre in Srebrenica in July 1995, which resulted in the
6 systematic killing of over 8,000 Bosniak victims, has been recognized as an act of
7 genocide by the International Criminal Tribunal for the former Yugoslavia; and
8 WHEREAS, Kentucky has become home to many Bosnians who fled due to the
9 war and ethnic cleansing which took place in the 1990s and who have become integral
10 members of communities in the Commonwealth; and
11 WHEREAS, many members of Kentucky's Bosnian community are survivors or
12 descendants of survivors of the genocide, carrying with them memories of unimaginable
13 loss and pain; and
14 WHEREAS, the Commonwealth of Kentucky seeks to promote awareness of
15 genocides, support educational efforts, and foster a commitment to preventing similar
16 acts from occurring in the future;
17 NOW, THEREFORE,
18 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
19 Kentucky:
20 Section 1. The members recognize the importance of acknowledgment and
21 remembrance of such atrocities in honoring the victims and ensuring that such events are
22 never forgotten.
23 Section 2. The members hereby recognize July 11, 2025, as Remember
24 Srebrenica Day in the Commonwealth.
25 Section 3. The Clerk of the Senate is directed to transmit a copy of this
26 Resolution to Senator Mike Wilson for delivery.
