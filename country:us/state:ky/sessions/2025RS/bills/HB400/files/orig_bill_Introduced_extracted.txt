Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 1422
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 519.070 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) A person is guilty of tampering with a prisoner monitoring device when he or she
5 intentionally alters, disables, deactivates, tampers with, removes, damages, or
6 destroys any:
7 (a) Device used to facilitate electronic monitoring or supervision of a person who
8 is on probation or parole, or has been ordered to wear a device as a condition
9 of pretrial release; or
10 (b) Video recording or video monitoring device within a correctional facility.
11 (2) Tampering with a prisoner monitoring device is a Class D felony.
