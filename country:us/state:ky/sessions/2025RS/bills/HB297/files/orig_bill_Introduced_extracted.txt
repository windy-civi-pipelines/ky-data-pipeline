UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 952
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 160.041 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) When a board of education of a[an independent] school district desires to have its
5 district merge with[become] a contiguous[part of the county] school district, it shall
6 by motion so record its desire in the minutes of the board. The board, or its
7 executive officer, shall convey this request to the[ county] board of education of the
8 contiguous school district. At its next regular meeting, or at a special meeting held
9 prior thereto, the[ county] board of education of the contiguous school district shall
10 consider the[pass upon this] request.
11 (2) (a) If the[ county] board of education receiving the request refuses, or the two (2)
12 boards of education cannot agree upon such a proposition of merger[ of the
13 independent district with the county district], the question of merger shall be
14 submitted to the qualified voters of the two (2) districts at the next regular
15 election if the question is filed with the county clerk not later than the second
16 Tuesday in August preceding the regular election.
17 (b)[(a)] If a majority of those voting on the question from each respective
18 school district favor merger, the school boards of the two (2) school districts
19 shall jointly develop a plan for adoption of the merger.
20 (c)[(b)] If the two (2) school boards cannot agree to the terms of merger within
21 sixty (60) days following the date of the regular election, the chief state school
22 officer shall develop the terms of the adoption of merger.
23 [(c) Notwithstanding subsection (2)(a) of this section, if the independent school
24 district cannot meet its current operating expenses from projected revenue and
25 if the two (2) school boards cannot agree to the terms of a merger, the
26 proposition of merger shall be submitted to the Kentucky Board of Education,
27 and the Kentucky Board of Education shall determine whether the two (2)

2 (d) Upon completion of the plan for adoption of the merger, whether prepared by
