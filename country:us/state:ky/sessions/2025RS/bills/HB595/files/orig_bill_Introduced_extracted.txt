2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 403.720 is amended to read as follows:
4 As used in KRS 403.715 to 403.785:
5 (1) "Domestic animal" means a dog, cat, or other animal that is domesticated and kept
6 as a household pet, but does not include animals normally raised for agricultural or
7 commercial purposes;
8 (2) "Domestic violence and abuse" means:
9 (a) Physical injury, serious physical injury, stalking, sexual assault, strangulation,
10 assault, or the infliction of fear of imminent physical injury, serious physical
11 injury, sexual assault, strangulation, or assault between family members or
12 members of an unmarried couple;[ or]
13 (b) A pattern of physical, sexual, emotional, economic, psychological, or
14 technological actions or threats of actions between family members or
15 members of an unmarried couple that is used by one (1) person to gain or
16 maintain power and control over another, including intimidation,
17 manipulation, humiliation, isolation, and coercion; or
18 (c) Any conduct prohibited under[by] KRS 525.125, 525.130, 525.135, or
19 525.137, or the infliction of fear of such imminent conduct, taken against a
20 domestic animal when used as a method of coercion, control, punishment,
21 intimidation, or revenge directed against a family member or member of an
22 unmarried couple who has a close bond of affection to the domestic animal;
23 (3) "Family member" means a spouse, including a former spouse, a grandparent, a
24 grandchild, a parent, an adult sibling, a child, a stepchild, or any other person living
25 in the same household as a child if the child is the alleged victim;
26 (4) "Foreign protective order" means any judgment, decree, or order of protection
27 which is entitled to full faith and credit pursuant to 18 U.S.C. sec. 2265 that was

1 issued on the basis of domestic violence and abuse;
2 (5) "Global positioning monitoring system" means a system that electronically
3 determines a person's location through a device worn by the person which does not
4 invade his or her bodily integrity and which transmits the person's latitude and
5 longitude data to a monitoring entity;
6 (6) "Member of an unmarried couple" means each member of an unmarried couple
7 which allegedly has a child in common, any children of that couple, or a member of
8 an unmarried couple who are living together or have formerly lived together;
9 (7) "Order of protection" means an emergency protective order or a domestic violence
10 order and includes a foreign protective order;

12 sexual abuse under KRS Chapter 510 or incest under KRS 530.020, or a criminal
13 attempt, conspiracy, facilitation, or solicitation to commit rape, sodomy, sexual
14 abuse, or incest;

17 the crime of stalking;
18 (10) "Strangulation" refers to conduct prohibited under[by] KRS 508.170 or[and]
19 508.175, or a criminal attempt, conspiracy, facilitation, or solicitation to commit the
20 crime of strangulation; and
21 (11)[(10)] "Substantial violation" means criminal conduct which involves actual or
22 threatened harm to the person, family, or property, including a domestic animal, of
23 an individual protected by an order of protection.
