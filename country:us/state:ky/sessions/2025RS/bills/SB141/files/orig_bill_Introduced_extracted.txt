UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 1658
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 36.230 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) There shall be a Unit of Civil Air Patrol in the Department of Military Affairs. The
5 unit shall consist of the Kentucky wing of the Civil Air Patrol and any other units
6 and personnel as are added by administrative order.
7 (2) The commanding officer or his or her designee of the Kentucky wing of the Civil
8 Air Patrol shall be ex officio the head of the unit.
