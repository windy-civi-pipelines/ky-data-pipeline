1 A RESOLUTION urging the executive branch of the Commonwealth of Kentucky
2 to reinstate the authority of the Capitol Annex Snack Bar to prepare and serve fried
3 bologna sandwiches.
4 WHEREAS, citizens of the United States and the Commonwealth of Kentucky are
5 guaranteed the right of life, liberty, and the pursuit of happiness; and
6 WHEREAS, the blessings of joy are often difficult to find in difficult and trying
7 situations; and
8 WHEREAS, for years, occupants and visitors of the Capitol Annex have found
9 consolation through the loving preparations of the Annex Snack Bar; and
10 WHEREAS, we hold these truths to be self-evident, that over the decades, the
11 momentary joy of partaking in the simple pleasure of a fried bologna sandwich is a right
12 that should not be withheld from the people; and
13 WHEREAS, recent government interference and gross overreach have denied these
14 small mercies by prohibiting the preparation of those blessed fried bologna sandwiches;
15 NOW, THEREFORE,
16 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
17 Kentucky:
18 Section 1. The Senate urges the executive branch of the Commonwealth of
19 Kentucky to reverse its misguided prohibition of the preparation of fried bologna
20 sandwiches and restore this simple and necessary pleasure to the occupants and visitors
21 of the Capitol Annex.
22 Section 2. The Clerk of the Senate is directed to transmit a copy of this
23 Resolution to Governor Andy Beshear, State Capitol, Room 100, Frankfort, Kentucky
24 40601.
