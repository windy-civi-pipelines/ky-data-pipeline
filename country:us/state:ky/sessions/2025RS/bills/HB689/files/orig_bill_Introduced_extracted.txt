Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 1891
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 335B IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) A person shall not use a title, term, or abbreviation to imply that the person is
6 licensed or certified to practice a profession, or otherwise hold himself or herself
7 out to the public as licensed, or certified to practice that profession, unless the
8 person holds a valid license or certification.
9 (2) A violation of subsection (1) of this Act is a Class B misdemeanor.
