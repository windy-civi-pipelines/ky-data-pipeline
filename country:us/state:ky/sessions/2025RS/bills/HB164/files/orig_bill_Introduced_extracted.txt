Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 385
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 199 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) In any pending adoption proceeding under this chapter, the court may enter a
6 judgment of adoption after a child's death upon the motion of the petitioner or
7 petitioners if:
8 (a) The child dies during the time the child is placed in the home of the
9 petitioner or petitioners;
10 (b) The court has received the report of the guardian ad litem, if any, for the
11 child and the report required by KRS 199.510;
12 (c) A hearing has been scheduled and notice has been given as required under
13 KRS 199.515; and
14 (d) The court determines after hearing the case the requirements of KRS
15 199.520(1) have been met.
16 (2) Upon entry of any judgment of adoption under this section, the provisions of KRS
17 199.520(1) related to the judgment and KRS 199.520(2) and (3) shall apply.
18 Section 2. This Act may be cited as Braylon's Law.
