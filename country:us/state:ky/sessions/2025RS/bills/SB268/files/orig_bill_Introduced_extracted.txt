UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 1369
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. Until June 30, 2026, the commissioner of the Kentucky Department
4 of Education, the Kentucky Board of Education, and the Kentucky Department of
5 Education shall not establish or implement, or require a school district to implement, any
6 cap, limitation, or restriction on enrollment for a virtual program. Additionally, the
7 Kentucky Department of Education and the Kentucky Board of Education shall not
8 reduce or withhold any funds due to a school district from the fund to Support Education
9 Excellence in Kentucky based on the district's operation of a virtual program.
