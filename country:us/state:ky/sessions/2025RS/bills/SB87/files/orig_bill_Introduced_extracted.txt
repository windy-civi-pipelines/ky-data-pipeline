4 (1) The ["]Kentucky aviation economic development fund["] is established in the State
5 Treasury. Beginning on July 1, 2000, all receipts collected under KRS Chapter 139

7 (2) The fund may also receive state appropriations, gifts, grants, and federal funds and
8 shall include earnings from investments of moneys from the fund.

13 or operated aviation facilities and for other aviation programs within the
14 Commonwealth that will benefit publicly owned or operated aviation facilities.

17 administrative costs incurred by the cabinet or for any purpose other than the
18 development, rehabilitation, and maintenance of publicly owned or operated
19 aviation facilities and other aviation programs benefiting publicly owned or
20 operated aviation facilities.
21 (6) The cabinet shall, no later than October 1 of each year, submit a report on the
22 use of funds in the Kentucky aviation economic development fund for the
23 previous fiscal year to the General Assembly through the Legislative Research
24 Commission.
