3 WHEREAS, Ashley Gorley is a native son of the Commonwealth of Kentucky,

5 WHEREAS, Ashley Gorley is the most commercially successful songwriter of all

7 WHEREAS, Ashley Gorley has written nearly 500 songs for many well-known
8 artists, including: Morgan Wallen, Chris Stapleton, Carrie Underwood, Luke Bryan, Post

14 poignant "I Am Not Okay," written with and recorded by Jelly Roll, depicts the

16 provides a platform for further mental health advocacy for fans and members of the
17 music community alike; and

20 Night," reigned as Billboard’s Top Hot 100 Song for a record-setting 16 weeks, proving
21 to be one of the biggest country crossover hits of all time; and
