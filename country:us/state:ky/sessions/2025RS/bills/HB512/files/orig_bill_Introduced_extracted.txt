UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 1690
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 501.080 is amended to read as follows:
4 Intoxication is a defense to a criminal charge only if such condition either:
5 (1) Negates[Negatives] the existence of an element of the offense; or
6 (2) Is not voluntarily produced and deprives the defendant of substantial capacity either
7 to appreciate the criminality of his or her conduct or to conform his or her conduct
8 to the requirements of the law.
