Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 1851
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 278.610 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) The Public Service Commission may certify a nuclear power facility if it finds that
5 the facility and plan for storage of the facility's high-level nuclear waste have been
6 approved by the Nuclear Regulatory Commission.
7 (2) The commission may[shall have the authority to] hire a consultant to perform duties
8 relating to this section. Any expenses or fees incurred by the commission in hiring a
9 consultant shall be borne by the applicant.
10 (3) The construction of low-level nuclear waste disposal sites in the Commonwealth
11 shall be prohibited, except as provided in KRS 211.852.
