3 WHEREAS, Dr. Robert "Bob" L. Jackson, a native of LaRue County, was
4 appointed as the 14th president of Murray State University on March 1, 2019, after

9 WHEREAS, under Dr. Jackson’s leadership as president of Murray State

11 freshman class for Fall 2023, and the 4th largest freshman class for Fall 2024, among
12 other enrollment successes; and
13 WHEREAS, Dr. Jackson's administrative team ensured access and affordability for

16 institution, receiving record rankings in this area for years; and

22 Chronicle of Higher Education; and

25 We Know," authored by Dr. Jackson; and
