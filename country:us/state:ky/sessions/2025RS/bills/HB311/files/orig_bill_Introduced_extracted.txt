2 WHEREAS, the General Assembly declares that there is a great need for living

10 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 141 IS CREATED TO
11 READ AS FOLLOWS:
12 (1) As used in this section:
13 (a) 1. "Leave of absence period" means the period, not exceeding the hourly
14 equivalent of forty (40) hours for each living donor, during which an
15 employer provides a paid leave of absence to a living donor for the
16 specific purpose of organ or bone marrow donation.
17 2. "Leave of absence period" does not include a period during which a
18 living donor utilizes any annual or sick leave that the living donor has
19 been granted by the employer;
20 (b) "Living donor" means an employee who is a Kentucky resident and is
21 absent from work solely for the purpose of donating an organ or bone
22 marrow; and
23 (c) "Organ" means any part of a human intestine, kidney, liver, lung,
24 pancreas, or uterus.
25 (2) For taxable years beginning on or after January 1, 2026, but before January 1,
26 2030, there is hereby established a nonrefundable employers' organ and bone
27 marrow donation tax credit.
