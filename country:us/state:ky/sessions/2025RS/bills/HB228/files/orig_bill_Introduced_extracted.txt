Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 240
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 600 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 A child incarcerated pursuant to the statewide detention program established in KRS
6 15A.305 shall have the right to:
7 (1) Be kept safe, informed, and free from emotional, psychological, verbal, and
8 physical abuse;
9 (2) Speak with, see, and touch their family;
10 (3) Access appropriate medical, mental health, and behavioral health care;
11 (4) An education meeting their individual needs;
12 (5) A nutritious and healthy diet;
13 (6) Be treated with respect and recognition of their dignity and individuality;
14 (7) Be free from unreasonable use of seclusion and restraint;
15 (8) Receive notice of and participate in court hearings;
16 (9) Access counsel; and
17 (10) Be free from judgment, blame, or stigma associated with detention.
18 Section 2. This Act may be cited as the Incarcerated Children's Bill of Rights.
