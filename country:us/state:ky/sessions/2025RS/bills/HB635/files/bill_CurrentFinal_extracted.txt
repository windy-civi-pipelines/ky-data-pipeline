UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS HB 635/GA
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 42 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section:
6 (a) "Agency" means any state or local governmental department, cabinet,
7 institution, board, commission, office, or any other unit of government; and
8 (b) "Grant" means money, equipment, material, or services that may be
9 available to an individual or entity from any agency through an application
10 process administered by the agency.
11 (2) The Finance and Administration Cabinet shall establish a grant database that
12 shall serve as a single, unified entry point for individuals and entities to access
13 and review grant opportunities that are eligible for application. The database
14 shall:
15 (a) Include the ability to use keyword searches to discover all available grant
16 opportunities; and
17 (b) Provide in the information section of the related grant all information
18 reported by agencies as required under subsection (4) of this section.
19 (3) The primary goals of the grant database include:
20 (a) Ensuring state and local grants are easily accessible for individuals and
21 entities of the Commonwealth; and
22 (b) Clearly stating requirements for grants to ensure submissions are qualified
23 for the potential grant.
24 (4) Beginning December 1, 2025, all agencies shall report quarterly to the Finance
25 and Administration Cabinet the grant opportunities that are currently available
26 from their agency. The report shall include:
27 (a) The title of the grant;

2 (c) The posting date of the grant;

8 (h) Any other information that may assist in the identification and orderly

11 database prominently on the homepage or first page of its website.
