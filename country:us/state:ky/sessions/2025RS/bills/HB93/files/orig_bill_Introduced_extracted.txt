Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 189
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 237 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section:
6 (a) "Firearm" has the same meaning as in KRS 237.060;
7 (b) "Handgun" has the same meaning as in KRS 237.060; and
8 (c) 1. "Target shooting" means the recreational use of a firearm or
9 handgun on targets.
10 2. "Target shooting" shall not include the use of a firearm or handgun
11 for hunting as defined in KRS 150.010 or when the use of a firearm or
12 handgun is justified in accordance with KRS Chapter 503.
13 (2) A person shall not target shoot carelessly, recklessly, or without regard for the
14 safety of any person, or in a manner that endangers or is likely to endanger any
15 person, pet, livestock, wildlife, or property.
16 (3) It shall not be a violation of subsection (2) of this section if a person participates
17 in target shooting:
18 (a) At a recreation facility designed for target shooting; or
19 (b) In an area with an unobstructed, earthen backstop capable of stopping all
20 projectiles and debris in a safe manner.
21 (4) Any person who violates this section shall be guilty of a Class A misdemeanor.
