2 Kentucky 2A Boys’ State Basketball Tournament champions.
3 WHEREAS, on January 19, 2025, at the Owensboro Sportscenter, the Calloway
4 County High School Lakers defeated the Black Bears of Harlan County High School by a
5 score of 80 to 75 to capture the 2025 Kentucky 2A Boys’ State Basketball Championship;
6 and
7 WHEREAS, the championship game was close until the end, as the lead changed
8 eight times and neither team led by double figures; and
9 WHEREAS, early in the second half the Lakers trailed by nine points, before
10 completing a 17-8 run to take a 48 to 46 lead late in the third quarter. Leading 69 to 60 in
11 the fourth quarter, the Lakers withstood the Black Bears’ late push by making free throws
12 to seal the game; and
13 WHEREAS, the 2A State Championship is the first in school history for Calloway
14 County. The win was keyed by the tournament's Most Valuable Player Jonah Butler’s
15 team-high 36 points in the championship game and a 2A State Tournament record 84
16 points throughout the competition; and
17 WHEREAS, Jonah Butler’s father, Greg Butler, an assistant principal at Calloway
18 County, handed his son the MVP trophy after the game; and
19 WHEREAS, senior Eli Finley added 34 points for the Lakers, and sophomore Luke
20 Armstrong contributed 10 rebounds; and
21 WHEREAS, Butler, Finley, and Armstrong were joined by seniors Stephen Lane
22 and Cole Lockhart on the All-Tournament Team; and
23 WHEREAS, after the victory, Calloway County head coach Brad Cleaver said, "I
24 am so proud of my kids. They’ve been together for eight, nine years, playing together.
25 This just puts a ribbon on all of our hard work. They deserve this. They worked hard for
26 this, and I’m very, very proud of them"; and
27 WHEREAS, the victory in the 2A State Championship game is a highlight in an

12 and

17 NOW, THEREFORE,
18 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
19 Kentucky:
20 Section 1. The members of the Senate join Senator Jason Howell in
21 congratulating the Calloway County Lakers and Coach Brad Cleaver on winning the
22 2025 Kentucky 2A Boys’ State Basketball Championship, applauding their stellar play,
23 and wishing the team and coaches good fortune in the rest of their season and in all future
24 endeavors.
25 Section 2. The Clerk of the Senate shall transmit a copy of this Resolution to
26 Senator Jason Howell for presentation to the team.
