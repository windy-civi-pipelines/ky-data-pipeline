1 AN ACT relating to the regulation of nicotine products and declaring an
2 emergency.

4 Section 1. The provisions of KRS 241.060, 438.305, 438.306, 438.307,
5 438.308, 438.309, 438.310, 438.312, 438.313, 438.316, and 438.331 that were enacted in
6 2024 Ky. Acts ch. 111 are hereby suspended until April 15, 2026.
7 Section 2. This Act is retroactive to January 1, 2025.
8 Section 3. Whereas a decision by the Supreme Court of the United States on the
9 status of the Food and Drug Administration's denial of approval of flavored vaping
10 products is necessary before the Commonwealth imposes stricter regulations on
11 individuals and businesses in this state, an emergency is declared to exist, and this Act
12 takes effect upon its passage and approval by the Governor or upon its otherwise
13 becoming a law.
