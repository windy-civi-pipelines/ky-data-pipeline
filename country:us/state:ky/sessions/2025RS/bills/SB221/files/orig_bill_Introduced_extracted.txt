2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 335.320 is amended to read as follows:
4 The board shall:
5 (1) Administer and enforce the provisions of this chapter and shall evaluate the
6 qualifications of license and permit applicants;
7 (2) Approve the examination required of applicants for licensure, provide for the
8 administration and grading of the examination, and provide for other matters
9 relating to licensure in the profession of marriage and family therapy;
10 (3) Review the credentials of licensees to determine if they are eligible for license
11 renewal and have paid the fee provided for in KRS 335.340;
12 (4) License the marriage and family therapist applicants who satisfy the experience and
13 educational requirements of KRS 335.330 and have paid the fee provided for in
14 KRS 335.330;
15 (5) Review and approve contracts between marriage and family therapy associates and
16 approved supervisors for their supervision of practice during the qualifying term;
17 (6) Issue permits to marriage and family therapy associate applicants who satisfy the
18 requirements of KRS 335.332;
19 (7) Adopt a code of ethics for licensed marriage and family therapists and marriage and
20 family therapy associates;
21 (8) Submit an annual report to the Governor and to the Legislative Research
22 Commission by January 1 of each year, listing all hearings conducted by the board
23 and any decisions rendered;[ and]
24 (9) Promulgate administrative regulations, in accordance with KRS Chapter 13A, to
25 implement the purpose and scope of KRS 335.300 to 335.399; and
26 (10) Enter into reciprocal agreements with boards of marriage and family therapy in
27 other states having licensure qualifications and requirements that meet or exceed
