Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 1696
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS 383.200 TO 383.285 IS CREATED
4 TO READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section, "extreme weather conditions" means a:
6 (a) Day for which the National Weather Service has issued either an Extreme
7 Cold Warning or Excessive Heat Warning for the county in which the court
8 is located; or
9 (b) Period of natural disaster in the county in which the court is located. As
10 used in this paragraph, "natural disaster" means any natural disaster
11 resulting in the declaration of a state of emergency under KRS 39A.100 or
12 39B.070.
13 (2) The court shall stay the execution of a warrant of restitution of a residential
14 property, from day to day, during extreme weather conditions.
15 (3) When a stay has been granted under subsection (2) of this section, the execution
16 of the warrant of restitution for which the stay has been granted shall be given
17 priority and completed within three (3) days after the extreme weather conditions
18 cease.
19 Section 2. KRS 383.245 is amended to read as follows:

23 plaintiff, it shall also, subject to Section 1 of this Act, issue a warrant of restitution in
