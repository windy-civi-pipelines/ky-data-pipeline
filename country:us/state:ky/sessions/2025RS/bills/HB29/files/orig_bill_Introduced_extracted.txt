2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 227.480 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) (a) A city, county, urban-county government, charter county, or consolidated
5 local government or the state shall, according to the Uniform State Building
6 Code as it pertains to the plan review and inspection responsibilities of local
7 governments or the state, require any person to obtain a permit before
8 commencing construction, alteration, or repairs of any electrical system.
9 (b) The city, county, urban-county government, charter county, or consolidated
10 local government or the state shall require all inspections that are deemed
11 necessary by the department for the safety of life and property. The
12 department shall promulgate administrative regulations to describe the
13 circumstances where inspections are required.
14 (2) A city, county, urban-county government, charter county, or consolidated local
15 government or the state shall not issue a permit unless the applicant submits proof
16 of being licensed as an electrical contractor under KRS Chapter 227A or of acting
17 on behalf of a licensed electrical contractor. However, the provisions of this
18 subsection shall not apply to:
19 (a) A homeowner or farmer who does construction, alteration, or repairs of any
20 electrical system on his or her own premises or any other person exempt from
21 licensing under KRS 227A.030 or 227A.150;
22 (b) Electrical work performed by the Commonwealth of Kentucky, a city, county,
23 urban-county government, charter county, or consolidated local government,
24 or any subdivision thereof;
25 (c) A company with a recently deceased licensed electrical contractor, which
26 shall be granted an interim period of up to one hundred eighty (180)
27 continuous calendar days by the city, county, urban-county government,
