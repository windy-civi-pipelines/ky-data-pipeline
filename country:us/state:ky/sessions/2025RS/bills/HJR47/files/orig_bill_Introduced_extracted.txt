3 WHEREAS, Venita Carol Morgan Caldwell was born on August 29, 1953, in

5 WHEREAS, Venita Caldwell attended Eastern Kentucky University to study

7 WHEREAS, Venita Caldwell dedicated her life to education and had a passion for

11 for 40 years, where she taught night classes; and
12 WHEREAS, Venita Caldwell was known for her kind and welcoming personality,
13 and had a talent for engaging conversations and storytelling that left lasting impacts on
14 those who knew her; and
15 WHEREAS, Venita Caldwell passed away on June 10, 2024, and is survived by her
16 loving husband, Doug Caldwell; her son, Ben Caldwell; and countless other family
17 members and friends; and
18 WHEREAS, Venita Caldwell is remembered as a loving wife, mother, and
19 dedicated educator, and her contributions to education deserve tangible recognition;
20 NOW, THEREFORE,
21 Be it resolved by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
22 Section 1. The Transportation Cabinet shall designate the portion of Kentucky
23 Route 80 in Leslie County, from its intersection with Daisy Lane to its intersection with
24 Pool Drive, as the "Venita Caldwell Memorial Highway" and shall, within 30 days of the
25 effective date of this Resolution, erect appropriate signage denoting this designation.
