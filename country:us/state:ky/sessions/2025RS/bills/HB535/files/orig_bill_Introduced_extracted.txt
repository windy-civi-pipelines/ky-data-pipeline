UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 1243
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 186.032 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) As used in this section, "communication disorder" means a health condition or
5 disability that may impede effective communication with a peace officer,
6 including:
7 (a) A communication impediment;
8 (b) Blindness or a visual impairment;
9 (c) Autism spectrum disorder;
10 (d) A drug allergy;
11 (e) Alzheimer's disease or dementia;
12 (f) Post-traumatic stress disorder;
13 (g) Traumatic brain injury;
14 (h) Schizophrenia;
15 (i) Epilepsy;
16 (j) A developmental disability;
17 (k) Down syndrome;
18 (l) Diabetes; or
19 (m) A heart condition.
20 The Transportation Cabinet may identify other communication disorders through
21 administrative regulations promulgated in accordance with KRS Chapter 13A.
22 (2) The owner or lessee of a motor vehicle may inform the county clerk that he or she,
23 [or ]someone who may be operating the vehicle, or someone who is a regular
24 passenger in the vehicle is deaf or hard of hearing and request that information be
25 included in the Kentucky vehicle registration system database to assist law
26 enforcement in identifying the operator or a regular passenger of the vehicle as
27 possibly being deaf or hard of hearing.

1 (3) The owner or lessee of a motor vehicle may inform the county clerk that he or she,
2 [or ]someone who may be operating the vehicle, or someone who is a regular
3 passenger in the vehicle has a communication disorder and request that information
4 be included in the Kentucky vehicle registration system database to assist law
5 enforcement in identifying the operator or a regular passenger of the vehicle as
6 possibly having a communication disorder.
7 (4) (a) The deaf or hard of hearing protection trust fund is created as a separate trust
8 fund in the State Treasury. The trust fund shall consist of any proceeds from
9 gifts, grants, contributions, appropriations, or other moneys made available
10 for the purposes of the trust fund.
11 (b) The fund shall be administered by the Kentucky Commission on the Deaf or
12 Hard of Hearing.
13 (c) Notwithstanding KRS 45.229, trust fund amounts not expended at the close of
14 a fiscal year shall not lapse but shall be carried forward to the next fiscal year.
15 (d) Any interest earnings of the trust fund shall become part of the trust fund and
16 shall not lapse.
17 (e) Trust fund moneys deposited in this fund shall only be used to:
18 1. Reimburse the Transportation Cabinet for the cost of including
19 information that someone is deaf or hard of hearing or has a
20 communication disorder in the Kentucky vehicle registration system
21 database;
22 2. Support other actions to protect the safety and welfare of persons who
23 are deaf or hard of hearing or have a communication disorder; and
24 3. Educate the public, the deaf and hard of hearing community, and the
25 communication disorder community on issues confronting the deaf and
26 hard of hearing and those with communication disorders.
27 (5) (a) Every county clerk shall post a permanent notice containing information about
