3 WHEREAS, Burlin Coleman was born on March 9, 1929, in Pike County,

5 WHEREAS, Burlin Coleman grew up on a hillside farm in eastern Kentucky and

2 WHEREAS, Burlin Coleman served as a trustee and chairman of the board of the
