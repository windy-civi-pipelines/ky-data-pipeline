4 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 411 IS CREATED TO
5 READ AS FOLLOWS:
6 (1) As used in this section, "minor" means a person under twenty-one (21) years of
7 age.
8 (2) Any person who sustains injury or property damage, or the estate of any person
9 killed, as a proximate result of the negligence of an intoxicated minor shall have,
10 in addition to any other cause of action available in tort, a cause of action
11 against:
12 (a) Any person who knew or should have known through the use of ordinary
13 care that a minor consumed alcohol or another intoxicating or controlled
14 substance not medically prescribed to the minor in the person's home or on
15 property under the person's control; and
16 (b) Any person, other than the minor's parent, who furnished, delivered, or
17 procured for the minor the alcohol or other intoxicating or controlled
18 substance not medically prescribed to the minor.
