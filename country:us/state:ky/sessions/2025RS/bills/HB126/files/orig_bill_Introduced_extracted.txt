Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 454
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 2.110 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) The first day of January (New Year's Day), the third Monday of January (Birthday
5 of Martin Luther King, Jr.), the nineteenth day of January (Robert E. Lee Day), the
6 thirtieth day of January (Franklin D. Roosevelt Day), the twelfth day of February
7 (Lincoln's Birthday), the third Monday in February (Washington's Birthday), the
8 last Monday in May (Memorial Day), the third day of June (Confederate Memorial
9 Day, and Jefferson Davis Day), the nineteenth day of June (Juneteenth National
10 Freedom Day), the fourth day of July (Independence Day), the first Monday in
11 September (Labor Day), the second Monday in October (Columbus Day), the
12 eleventh day of November (Veterans Day), the twenty-fifth day of December
13 (Christmas Day) of each year, and all days appointed by the President of the United
14 States or by the Governor as days of thanksgiving, are holidays, on which all the
15 public offices of this Commonwealth may be closed.
16 (2) No person shall be compelled to labor on the first Monday in September (Labor
17 Day) by any person.
