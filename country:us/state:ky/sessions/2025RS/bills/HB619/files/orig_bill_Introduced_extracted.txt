UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 1916
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 156.690 is amended to read as follows:
4 The Kentucky Board of Education shall initiate a program to assist and encourage each
5 certified teacher to purchase a computer for his or her own personal use. The program
6 shall include[,] but not be limited to[,] the following:
7 (1) The Kentucky Board of Education shall obtain by competitive bidding or
8 negotiation the lowest possible purchase prices for various personal computers on
9 behalf of all interested teachers.
10 (2) The Kentucky Board of Education shall arrange a suitable training program in the
11 use of these computers with the vendor awarded the contract.
