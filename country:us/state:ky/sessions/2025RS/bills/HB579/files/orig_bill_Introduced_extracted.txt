Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 1101
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 198B IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 The Kentucky Building Code shall permit less than six (6) stories of Residential Group
6 R-2 occupancy, as defined in the building code, to be served by a single exit if:
7 (1) The building does not:
8 (a) Have more than six (6) stories above grade plane; and
9 (b) Contain more than four (4) dwelling units on any floor;
10 (2) An exterior or interior stairway is provided;
11 (3) (a) A corridor separates each dwelling unit entry or exit door from the door to
12 an interior exit stairway, including any related exit passageway on each
13 floor; and
14 (b) The dwelling unit doors do not open directly into an interior exit stairway,
15 but may open directly into an exterior stairway;
16 (4) There is no more than twenty (20) feet of travel to the exit stairway from the entry
17 or exit door of any dwelling unit;
18 (5) Travel distance measured in accordance with the building code does not exceed
19 one hundred twenty-five (125) feet;
20 (6) Other occupancies in the same building do not have access to the Residential
21 Group R-2 occupancy portion of the building or with the single-exit stairway.
22 This shall not include parking garages and occupied roofs that are part of the
23 Residential Group R-2 occupancy that may have access to the exit stairway;
24 (7) The exit serving the Residential Group R-2 occupancy does not discharge
25 through any other occupancy, including an attached parking garage; and
26 (8) There are no openings within ten (10) feet of unprotected openings into the
27 stairway other than required exit doors having a one (1) hour fire-resistance
