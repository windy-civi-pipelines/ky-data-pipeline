1 Be it resolved by the House of Representatives of the General Assembly of the
2 Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. The General Assembly urges the United States Army Corps of
4 Engineers to assist with and allow for the dredging of rivers and creeks in Kentucky,
5 beginning with those in eastern Kentucky most affected by the recent flood waters, for
6 the prevention and mitigation of future potential flood damage in the Commonwealth.
7 Section 2. The General Assembly urges the United States Congress to introduce
8 and adopt legislation to assist Kentucky with flood prevention and mitigation, as well as
9 flood damage relief efforts, including but not limited to funding for the current disaster
10 recovery efforts in Kentucky and removing regulations and restrictions on dredging so
11 that the citizens of the Commonwealth may have the full right to clean up and dredge the
12 waters of Kentucky and landowners may put the dredged material to beneficial use on
13 their properties.
14 Section 3. The Clerk of the House of Representatives is directed to transmit a
15 copy of this Resolution to the Commander of the Louisville District of the United States
16 Army Corps of Engineers, the Speaker of the United States House of Representatives, the
17 Majority Leader of the United States Senate, and each member of the Kentucky
18 congressional delegation.
