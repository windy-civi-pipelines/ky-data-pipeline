2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 42.032 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) [By January 1, 2012, ]The Finance and Administration Cabinet shall provide public
5 access to records relating to expenditures of the executive branch of state
6 government through display of the records on a website[Web site]. The
7 website[Web site] shall provide:
8 (a) Financial information of expenditures not exempt under the provisions of state
9 or federal law, including:
10 1. The payee name;
11 2. The category or type of the expenditure;
12 3. A description of the reason for the expenditure, if available;
13 4. The expenditure amount; and
14 5. A link to the financial document, if the document is electronically
15 available;
16 (b) A searchable format;
17 (c) Access to the current enacted Executive Branch Budget; and
18 (d) A link to the public access website[Web site] displays of the legislative and
19 judicial branches of state government, and of the public institutions of higher
20 education.
21 (2) Information on the website[Web site] shall be updated at least [on a ]monthly[
22 basis]. However, information on the website[Web site] which is part of, or
23 contained in, an electronic accounting system utilized by all branches of state
24 government, such as the Enhanced Management Administrative Reporting System
25 (EMARS), shall be updated [on a ]weekly[ basis].
26 (3) The cabinet shall maintain exclusive control and be considered the sole custodian of
27 all information and records generated by and through activity of the executive
