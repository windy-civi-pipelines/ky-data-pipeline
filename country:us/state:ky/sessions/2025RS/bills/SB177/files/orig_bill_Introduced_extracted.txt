Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 1691
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 214 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) Any COVID-19 vaccine, modified ribonucleic acid (modRNA) vaccine, or
6 messenger ribonucleic acid (mRNA) vaccine shall not be required for any
7 individual for the purposes of student enrollment, employment, or medical
8 treatment in the Commonwealth.
9 (2) No COVID-19 vaccine, modified ribonucleic acid (modRNA) vaccine, or
10 messenger ribonucleic acid (mRNA) vaccine shall be administered in the
11 Commonwealth to a minor child under the age of eighteen (18) years.
12 (3) The provisions of subsection (2) of this section that apply to a minor child under
13 the age of eighteen (18) years shall expire on July 1, 2035, unless extended by an
14 act of the General Assembly following legislative review of available safety data.
