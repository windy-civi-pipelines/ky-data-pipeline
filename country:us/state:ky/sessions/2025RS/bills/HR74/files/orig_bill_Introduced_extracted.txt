4 United States, intent on erecting a government that would stand the test of time and
5 protect the hard-won freedoms secured during the Revolutionary War, gathered at the
6 Pennsylvania State House in Philadelphia; and

8 remains the bedrock of our system of government, one rooted in equality under the law

11 divided political power among three separate and coequal branches, and further between

25 America by upholding our constitutional system, honoring its framers and those who
26 have sacrificed to defend it, and embracing the duty we as citizens have to preserve the
27 society it has built; and

1 WHEREAS, Constitution Day was established to celebrate the United States
2 Constitution and its Bill of Rights and provides an opportunity for American citizens to
3 reaffirm the achievements of our founding fathers and recommit ourselves to preserving
4 the rights, privileges, and responsibilities our nation's founders secured for themselves
5 and for the posterity of future generations of Americans; and
6 WHEREAS, the Commonwealth of Kentucky was admitted into the Union as the
7 fifteenth state on June 1, 1792; and
8 WHEREAS, the original 1792 Constitution of Kentucky established a framework
9 for governance, including a bicameral legislature, an executive branch, and a judicial
10 system, while incorporating a bill of rights; and
11 WHEREAS, the Commonwealth has since adopted subsequent constitutions in
12 1799, 1850, and the current constitution in 1891, each reflecting the evolving values and
13 principles of its citizens; and
14 WHEREAS, the Constitution of Kentucky has played a pivotal role in shaping the
15 state's legal and cultural identity, ensuring the rights and liberties of its people; and
16 WHEREAS, recognizing June 1, 2025, as Kentucky Constitution Day offers an
17 opportunity for citizens to reflect upon and celebrate the rich constitutional heritage of
18 the Commonwealth;
19 NOW, THEREFORE,
20 Be it resolved by the House of Representatives of the General Assembly of the
21 Commonwealth of Kentucky:
22 Section 1. The House of Representatives hereby recognizes September 17,
23 2025, as United States Constitution Day to celebrate our representative republic system
24 of government and the central contributions of the United States Constitution to the
25 freedom Americans are privileged to enjoy.
26 Section 2. The House of Representatives hereby recognizes June 1, 2025, as
27 Kentucky Constitution Day to honor the Commonwealth's constitutional heritage and the
