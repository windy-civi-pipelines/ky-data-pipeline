4 Section 1. Are you in favor of amending Section 1 of the Constitution of

9 natural resources for the benefit of all people, as stated below?
10 Section 2. It is proposed that Section 1 of the Constitution of Kentucky be
11 amended to read as follows:
12 All men are, by nature, free and equal, and have certain inherent and inalienable
13 rights, among which may be reckoned:
14 First: The right of enjoying and defending their lives and liberties.
15 Second: The right of worshipping Almighty God according to the dictates of their
16 consciences.
17 Third: The right of seeking and pursuing their safety and happiness.
18 Fourth: The right of freely communicating their thoughts and opinions.
19 Fifth: The right of acquiring and protecting property.
20 Sixth: The right of assembling together in a peaceable manner for their common
21 good, and of applying to those invested with the power of government for redress of
22 grievances or other proper purposes, by petition, address or remonstrance.
23 Seventh: The right to bear arms in defense of themselves and of the State, subject to
24 the power of the General Assembly to enact laws to prevent persons from carrying
25 concealed weapons.
26 Eighth: The right to a healthy environment, including a right to clean air, pure
27 water, and ecologically healthy habitats. The Commonwealth's natural resources,

9 Section 4. Notwithstanding any provision of KRS 118.415 to the contrary, the
10 Secretary of State shall cause the entirety of the question in Section 1 of this Act and the
11 entirety of the proposed amendment to the Constitution of Kentucky contained in Section
12 2 of this Act to be published at least one time in a newspaper of general circulation
13 published in this state, and shall also cause to be published at the same time and in the
14 same manner the fact that the amendment will be submitted to the voters for their
15 acceptance or rejection at the next regular election at which members of the General
16 Assembly are to be voted for. The publication required by this section and KRS 118.415
17 shall be made no later than the first Tuesday in August preceding the election at which
18 the amendment is to be voted on.

22 chosen in a year in which there is not an election for President and Vice President of the

24 preceding a regular election in a year in which there is an election for President and Vice
25 President of the United States, shall certify the entirety of the question in Section 1 of this
26 Act and the entirety of the proposed amendment to the Constitution of Kentucky
27 contained in Section 2 of this Act to the county clerk of each county, and the county clerk
