Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 491
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 335B IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) Each licensing authority listed in subsection (4) of this section shall collect the
6 following data using a data collection application, during the annual, biennial, or
7 triennial licensure renewal process:
8 (a) Licensee's personal information, including but not limited to:
9 1. The licensee's first, middle, and last name;
10 2. Email address and phone number;
11 3. Physical address of all practice locations;
12 4. License type;
13 5. License number and date of issuance;
14 6. License expiration; and
15 7. National Provider Identifier number; and
16 (b) Workforce participation information, including but not limited to:
17 1. Degree level or certificates completed;
18 2. Employment type;
19 3. Employment specialty;
20 4. Employment setting;
21 5. Employment status, including but not limited to:
22 a. Actively employed in respective field;
23 b. Actively employed in a clinical setting, either in person or
24 through telehealth, or both;
25 c. Actively employed outside of respective field;
26 d. Unemployed but seeking work in respective field; or
27 e. Unemployed but not seeking work in respective field;

1 6. Reason for unemployment; and
2 7. Ability to meet patient needs of those covered under the Federal
3 Americans with Disabilities Act.
4 (2) Data collected in subsection (1) of this section shall not include a licensee's
5 Social Security number.
6 (3) Licensees may notify the licensing authority if the licensee wishes to decline the
7 use of data collected in subsection (1)(a) of this section to be made available to
8 other employers or agencies for free or for purchase.
9 (4) The following licensing authorities are subject to the requirements of this section:
10 (a) Professional licensure boards as defined in KRS 211.332;
11 (b) Kentucky Board for Medical Imaging and Radiation Therapy established in
12 KRS 311B.030;
13 (c) State Board of Podiatry established in KRS 311.410; and
14 (d) Kentucky Department for Public Health, in regards to community health
15 workers certified under KRS 309.462.
16 (5) Each licensing authority listed in subsection (4) of this section shall report as
17 necessary, and upon request, the data collected in subsection (1) of this section to
18 the Office of the Kentucky Center for Statistics established in KRS 151B.132 and
19 the Cabinet for Health and Family Services for workforce capacity and workforce
20 reporting and tracking purposes.
21 SECTION 2. A NEW SECTION OF KRS 335.300 TO 335.399 IS CREATED
22 TO READ AS FOLLOWS:
23 (1) The board shall grant licensure under KRS 335.330 to an applicant who:
24 (a) Holds an active license from another state to practice as an independent
25 marriage and family therapist;
26 (b) Is in good standing with the certifying or licensing board of that state;
27 (c) Is of good moral character;
Page 2 of 3

UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 491
1 (d) Has not had disciplinary actions of a serious nature brought against him or
2 her by a registration, certification, or licensing board or agency;
3 (e) Has met the examination requirement set out in KRS 335.330(3); and
4 (f) Has paid the application fee to the board.
5 (2) The applicant shall be exempt from:
6 (a) The required education set out in KRS 335.330(1); and
7 (b) The required hours of supervised experience in the practice of marriage and
8 family therapy set out in KRS 335.330(2).
9 Section 3. Each licensing authority required to collect data in Section 1 of this
10 Act shall have until the licensing authority's next licensure renewal date following the
11 effective date of this Act to comply.
12 Section 4. Section 1 of this Act takes effect January 1, 2027.
