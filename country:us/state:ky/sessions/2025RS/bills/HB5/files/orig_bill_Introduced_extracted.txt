2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 15A IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 All incarcerated youth are subject to search at any time. Searches shall be conducted in
6 a professional and respectful manner, in the least intrusive manner possible, and
7 consistent with security needs.
8 SECTION 2. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 15A IS CREATED TO
9 READ AS FOLLOWS:
10 (1) As used in this section:
11 (a) "Changing room" means a room or area in which a person may be in a
12 state of undress in the presence of others, including a locker room or
13 shower room;
14 (b) "Female" means an individual whose biological reproductive system is
15 developed to produce ova; who has, had, will have or would have, but for a
16 developmental or genetic anomaly or historical accident, the reproductive
17 system that at some point produces, transports, and utilizes eggs for
18 fertilization;
19 (c) "Male" means an individual whose biological reproductive system is
20 developed to fertilize the ova of a female; who has, had, will have or would
21 have, but for a developmental or genetic anomaly or historical accident, the
22 reproductive system that at some point produces, transports, and utilizes
23 sperm for fertilization;
24 (d) "Sex" means an individual's biological sex, either male or female, as
25 observed or clinically verified at birth. Gender identity and other subjective
26 terms shall not be used as synonyms or substitutes for sex; and
27 (e) "Sleeping quarters" means a room with more than one (1) bed and in

1 which more than one (1) individual is housed overnight.
2 (2) A facility shall designate each multi-occupancy restroom, changing room, and
3 sleeping quarters for the exclusive use of either females, males, or members of
4 the same family.
5 (3) (a) A restroom, changing room, or sleeping quarters within a facility that is
6 designated for females or males shall be used only by members of that sex.
7 An individual shall not enter a restroom, changing room, or sleeping
8 quarters that is designated for females or males unless he or she is a
9 member of that sex.
10 (b) A facility shall take reasonable steps to provide individuals with privacy in
11 restrooms, changing rooms, and sleeping quarters from members of the
12 opposite sex.
13 (4) This section does not apply to an individual who enters a restroom, changing
14 room, or sleeping quarters designated for the opposite sex to:
15 (a) Perform custodial services or maintenance;
16 (b) Render medical assistance;
17 (c) Render assistance by law enforcement; or
18 (d) Provide services or render aid during a natural disaster, a declared
19 emergency, or when necessary to prevent a serious threat to good order or
20 safety.
21 (5) This section shall not be construed to prohibit a facility from adopting policies
22 necessary to accommodate persons protected under the Americans with
23 Disabilities Act when using restrooms, changing rooms, or sleeping quarters.

2 SECTION 4. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 197 IS CREATED TO
3 READ AS FOLLOWS:
4 (1) As used in this section:
5 (a) "Cosmetic service" or "elective procedure" means any procedure,
6 treatment, or surgery to enhance or alter appearance to any areas of the
7 head, neck, and body, including but not limited to:
8 1. Prescribing or administering cross-sex hormones in amounts greater
9 than would normally be produced endogenously in a healthy person of
10 the same age and sex; and
11 2. Performing any gender reassignment surgery to alter or remove
12 physical or anatomical characteristics or features that are typical for a
13 person’s sex in order to instill or create physiological or anatomical
14 characteristics that resemble a different sex;
15 (b) "Public funds" means any money, regardless of the original source of the
16 money, of:
17 1. The Commonwealth of Kentucky, and any department, agency, or
18 instrumentality thereof;
19 2. Any county, city, local school district, or special district, and any
20 department, agency, or instrumentality thereof; and
21 3. Any other political subdivision of the Commonwealth, and any
22 department, agency, or instrumentality thereof; and
23 (c) "Sex" means the biological indication of male and female as evidenced by
24 sex chromosomes, naturally occurring sex hormones, gonads, and
25 nonambiguous internal and external genitalia present at birth.
26 (2) Except as provided in subsection (3) of this section, public funds shall not be
27 directly or indirectly used, granted, paid, or distributed for the purpose of
