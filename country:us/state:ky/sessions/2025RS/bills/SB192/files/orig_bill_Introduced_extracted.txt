UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 1922
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 216B IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 Any county, charter county, urban-county government, consolidated local government,
6 or unified local government with a population of more than one hundred twenty
7 thousand (120,000) based on the most recent federal decennial census which is located
8 in a standard metropolitan statistical area with a population of more than two million
9 (2,000,000) based on the most recent federal decennial census and includes a state that
10 does not require a certificate of need for one (1) or more health services, may elect to
11 have every health service not requiring a certificate of need in the neighboring states
12 granted a nonsubstantive review pursuant to KRS 216B.095 and have the certificate of
13 need approved within thirty (30) days of receipt of submission for nonsubstantive
14 review.
15 Section 2. The following KRS section is repealed:
16 216B.010 Legislative findings and purposes.
