13 SECTION 2. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 434.550 TO 434.730 IS
14 CREATED TO READ AS FOLLOWS:
15 (1) A person is guilty of gift card tampering when he or she intentionally
16 manipulates a gift card’s packaging or security features to access the gift card’s
17 information without authorization and with the intention of using the
18 information to improperly access and utilize funds placed on the gift card by
19 another.
20 (2) Gift card tampering is a Class D felony.
21 (3) This section shall not apply to tampering with an activated gift card with or
22 without the consent of the cardholder.
23 (4) Conduct which may constitute a violation of this section may be used to show a
24 violation of KRS 506.120, but a person shall not be convicted of a violation of this
25 section and KRS 506.120.
