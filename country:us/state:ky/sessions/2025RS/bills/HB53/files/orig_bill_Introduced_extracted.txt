Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 871
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 512 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) A person is guilty of unlawful release of balloons when, within a twenty-four (24)
6 hour period, he or she intentionally releases, organizes the release of, or causes
7 to be released ten (10) or more balloons inflated with a gas that is lighter than air
8 except for:
9 (a) Balloons released on behalf of a governmental agency or pursuant to a
10 governmental contract for scientific or meteorological purposes;
11 (b) Hot air balloons that are recovered after launching; or
12 (c) Balloons released indoors.
13 (2) Unlawful release of balloons is a Class A misdemeanor.
