2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 197 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section:
6 (a) "Cosmetic service or elective procedure" means any procedure, treatment,
7 or surgery to enhance or alter the appearance of any area of the head, neck,
8 and body, including but not limited to:
9 1. Prescribing or administering cross-sex hormones in amounts greater
10 than would normally be produced endogenously in a healthy person of
11 the same age and sex; and
12 2. Performing any gender reassignment surgery to alter or remove
13 physical or anatomical characteristics or features that are typical for a
14 person’s sex in order to instill or create physiological or anatomical
15 characteristics that resemble a different sex;
16 (b) "Public funds" means any money, regardless of the original source of the
17 money, of:
18 1. The Commonwealth of Kentucky or any department, agency, or
19 instrumentality thereof;
20 2. Any county, city, local school district, or special district, or any
21 department, agency, or instrumentality thereof; and
22 3. Any other political subdivision of the Commonwealth or any
23 department, agency, or instrumentality thereof; and
24 (c) "Sex" means the biological indication of male and female as evidenced by
25 sex chromosomes, naturally occurring sex hormones, gonads, and
26 nonambiguous internal and external genitalia present at birth.
27 (2) Except as provided in subsection (3) of this section, public funds shall not be
