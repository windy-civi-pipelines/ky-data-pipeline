5 designated representatives, in the discharge of the duties of the secretary may:
6 1. Administer oaths and affirmations, take depositions, certify official acts,

17 the county in which the person is found, resides, or has his principal place of
18 business may, upon application of the secretary, his or her representative, or

20 proceeding brought under this section, the Circuit Court having issued the
21 order of compliance may modify or set aside the subpoena.
22 (c) Subpoenas issued under this section may be served by an authorized
23 representative of the cabinet, at any place in the state.

25 secretary's office to employees of the cabinet as he or she deems necessary and

9 appointed pursuant to law. The office shall be composed of the Division of
10 Program Policy and Support, the Division of Kentucky Business Enterprise, the
11 Division of Blind Services, the Division of Field Services, and the Division of the
12 Carl D. Perkins Vocational Training Center. Each division shall be headed by a
13 director appointed by the secretary of the Education and Labor Cabinet under the
14 provisions of KRS 12.050, and shall be composed of organizational entities as
15 deemed appropriate by the secretary of the Education and Labor Cabinet.
16 (2) The Office of Vocational Rehabilitation shall have such powers and duties as
17 contained in KRS 151B.180 to 151B.210 and KRS 163.450 to 163.480 and such
18 other functions as may be established by administrative regulation.
19 (3) The office shall be the sole state agency for the purpose of developing and
20 approving state plans required by state or federal laws and regulations as
21 prerequisites to receiving federal funds for vocational rehabilitation.
22 (4) The chief executive officer of the office shall be the executive director of the Office
23 of Vocational Rehabilitation. The executive director shall be appointed by the
24 secretary of the Education and Labor Cabinet under the provisions of KRS 12.050.
25 The executive director shall have experience in vocational rehabilitation and
26 supervision and shall have general supervision and direction over all functions of
27 the office and its employees, and shall be responsible for carrying out the programs

15 bureau, interstate compact, commission, committee, conference, council, office, or any
16 other form of organization shall be included in or attached to the department or program
17 cabinet in which they are included or to which they are attached by statute or statutorily
18 authorized executive order; except in the case of the Personnel Board and where the
