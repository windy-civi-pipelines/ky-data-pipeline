Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 1844
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 278.040 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) The Public Service Commission shall regulate utilities and enforce the provisions of
5 this chapter. The commission shall be a body corporate, with power to sue and be
6 sued in its corporate name. The commission may adopt a seal bearing the name
7 "Public Service Commission of Kentucky," which seal shall be affixed to all writs
8 and official documents, and to such other instruments as the commission directs,
9 and all courts shall take judicial note of the seal.
10 (2) The jurisdiction of the commission shall extend to all utilities in this state. The
11 commission shall have exclusive jurisdiction over the regulation of rates and
12 service of utilities, but with that exception nothing in this chapter is intended to
13 limit or restrict the police jurisdiction, contract rights, or powers of cities or
14 political subdivisions.
15 (3) The commission may adopt, in keeping with KRS Chapter 13A, reasonable
16 regulations to implement the provisions of KRS Chapter 278 and investigate the
17 methods and practices of utilities to require them to conform to the laws of this
18 state[,] and to all reasonable rules, regulations, and orders of the commission not
19 contrary to law.
