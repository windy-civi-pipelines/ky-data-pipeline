5 (1) As used in this section:

11 during the taxable year to occupy a rented dwelling within the

17 at or below one hundred thirty-three percent (133%) of the threshold
18 amount;

20 Commonwealth in which a renter lives for at least six (6) months out of the
21 year and includes:
22 1. Single-family houses;
23 2. Rooms within a single-family residence;
24 3. Apartments;
25 4. Duplexes;

4 qualified renter's family size.
5 (2) (a) For taxable years beginning on or after January 1, 2026, but before
6 January 1, 2030, a qualified renter may claim a refundable tax credit
7 against the tax imposed under KRS 141.020 for qualified rent payments
