7 primary or general election,[ except a current property valuation
8 administrator already qualified as a candidate to succeed himself or
9 herself in office,] or before that person may be appointed as a property
10 valuation administrator, the[except as an interim appointee as provided
11 by KRS 132.375, that] person shall hold a certificate issued by the
12 department[,] showing that he or she has been examined by the
13 department and is qualified for the office.
14 2. This section shall not apply to:
15 a. A current property valuation administrator already qualified as a
16 candidate to succeed himself or herself in office; or
17 b. An interim appointee as provided by KRS 132.375.
18 (b) All certificates issued shall expire one (1) year from the date of issuance.
19 (c) 1. The examinations shall be offered by the Kentucky Community and
20 Technical College System during the examination period.
21 2. The department shall provide the Kentucky Community and Technical
22 College System with the examination questions and other relevant
23 information for administering the examination.
24 3. The examination questions[examinations] shall be written and
25 formulated so as to test fairly the ability and fitness of the applicant to
26 serve as a property valuation administrator.
