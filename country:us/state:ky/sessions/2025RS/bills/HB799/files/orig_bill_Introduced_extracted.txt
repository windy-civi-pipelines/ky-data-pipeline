2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 11 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) Any law enacted by the General Assembly or program created by an act of the
6 General Assembly that directs an executive branch agency to perform a task or
7 provide a service shall be executed to the fullest extent by the agency.
8 (2) If the General Assembly does not provide additional funding or an appropriation
9 in its directive, then the agency shall be required to perform the task or provide
10 the service from its existing funds.
