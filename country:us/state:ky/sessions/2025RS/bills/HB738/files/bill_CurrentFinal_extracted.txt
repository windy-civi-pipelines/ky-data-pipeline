UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS HB 738/GA
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 64 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 Notwithstanding any provision of KRS Chapter 18A, KRS 64.640, or other law to the
6 contrary, constitutional officers specified in Section 93 of the Constitution of Kentucky
7 may:
8 (1) Employ persons exempt from the classified service as those officers deem
9 necessary or advisable for making and carrying out policy; and
10 (2) Increase by administrative order the salary of any classified employee with status
11 up to the midpoint of the grade for the employee's classification.
