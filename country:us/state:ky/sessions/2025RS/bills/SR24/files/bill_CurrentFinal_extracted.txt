3 WHEREAS, Dr. John Stempel graduated cum laude from Princeton University in

15 International Commerce at the University of Kentucky for 26 years. He served as

17 became a senior professor and was named a professor emeritus in 2013; and
18 WHEREAS, Dr. Stempel wrote two books, "Inside the Iranian Revolution" in 1981,
19 and "Common Sense and Foreign Policy" in 2008, and extensive articles relating to
20 foreign policy issues. He served as the editorial chairperson of the Foreign Service
21 Journal twice, and was a long-term member of the editorial board of the University Press
22 of Kentucky; and
23 WHEREAS, for decades, Dr. Stempel's wisdom and expertise served as the

25 WHEREAS, Dr. Stempel was a relentless advocate for the Patterson School and its

27 Lexington, and the Commonwealth of Kentucky; and
