4 A person certified after December 1, 1998, under KRS 15.380 to 15.404 or qualified

20 under this subsection expunged;

22 (8) Have received and read the Kentucky Law Enforcement Officers Code of Ethics as
23 established by the council;

25 discharge under other than honorable conditions, if having served in any branch of
26 the Armed Forces of the United States;
