UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS HB 132/GA
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 158.033 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) If in any district there are students not able even with the help of transportation to
5 be assembled in a school, instruction shall be provided to the student in the student's
6 home or in a hospital.
7 (2) For a student to be eligible for home or hospital instruction, a signed statement of
8 the diagnosed condition requiring home or hospital instruction shall be provided in
9 accordance with KRS 159.030(2).
10 (3) For a student admitted to an inpatient facility, the student may receive home or
11 hospital instruction effective on the day of admittance. Nothing in this subsection
12 shall exempt a board of education from the documentation requirements of KRS
13 159.030(2)(a).
14 (4) For the purposes of KRS 157.360, a student instructed under this section who
15 receives a minimum of two (2) instructional sessions a week with a minimum of
16 one (1) hour of instruction per session by a certified teacher provided by the board
17 of education shall equal the student attending five (5) days in school.
18 (5)[(4)] For students with disabilities, the admissions and release committee shall be
19 responsible for placement decisions regarding home or hospital instruction in
20 accordance with the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act, 20 U.S.C.
21 sec.[secs.] 1400 et. seq. The home or hospital instruction shall be provided pursuant
22 to the individual education program as determined by the admissions and release
23 committee. For the purposes of KRS 157.360, students receiving home or hospital
24 instruction under this subsection may be counted in attendance in accordance with
25 subsection (4)[(3)] of this section.
26 (6)[(5)] The Kentucky Board of Education shall promulgate administrative regulations
27 to establish the components of home or hospital instruction.
