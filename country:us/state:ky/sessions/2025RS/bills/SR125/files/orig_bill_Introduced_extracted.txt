1 WHEREAS, Senator Aaron Reed made history as the first Navy SEAL to serve in
2 the Kentucky Senate, bringing with him a depth of leadership experience and dedication
3 to the principles of honor, courage, and commitment; and
4 WHEREAS, since assuming office in 2025, Senator Aaron Reed has been a

6 vice chair of the Veterans, Military Affairs, and Public Protection Committee, as well as
7 contributing his expertise to the Judiciary, Education, and Agriculture Committees;
8 NOW, THEREFORE,
9 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
10 Kentucky:
11 Section 1. The Senate hereby recognizes and honors Senator Aaron Reed for his
12 distinguished military service and his legislative service, as the first Navy SEAL elected
13 to the Kentucky Senate.
14 Section 2. The Senate expresses its profound appreciation for Senator Aaron
15 Reed's leadership and experience as a Navy SEAL as he represents the people of
16 Kentucky, and extends its best wishes for his continued success in public service.
17 Section 3. The Clerk of the Senate is directed to transmit a copy of this
18 Resolution to Senator Aaron Reed as a token of gratitude and recognition for his service
19 and excellence.

11 then 24 year-old Aaron Reed answered the call to serve, fighting on the front lines with
12 unwavering courage and dedication; and
13 WHEREAS, throughout his distinguished military career, Senator Aaron Reed was
14 actively engaged in highly specialized and covert operations to combat zones, including
15 Iraq and Afghanistan, conducting hundreds of missions to uphold the values of freedom
16 and democracy; and

18 United States Navy as a Navy SEAL, attaining the rank of Senior Chief before his
19 retirement in December 2021; and

21 commitment to service by becoming a successful entrepreneur, founding and operating
