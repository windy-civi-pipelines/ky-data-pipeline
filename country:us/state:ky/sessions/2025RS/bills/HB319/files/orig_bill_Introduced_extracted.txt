3 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
4 Section 1. Are you in favor of amending Section 25 of the Constitution of
5 Kentucky to prohibit slavery and involuntary servitude in all circumstances, including as

9 amended to read as follows:
10 Slavery and involuntary servitude in this State are forbidden[, except as a

12 section shall be construed to prohibit a person convicted of a felony and sentenced to
13 imprisonment from being employed as described in Section 253 of this Constitution or
14 otherwise as may be provided by law.

16 Commonwealth for their ratification or rejection at the time and in the manner provided
17 for under Sections 256 and 257 of the Constitution, KRS 118.415, and Sections 4 and 5
18 of this Act.
19 Section 4. Notwithstanding any provision of in KRS 118.415 to the contrary,
20 the Secretary of State shall cause the entirety of the question in Section 1 of this Act and
21 the entirety of the proposed amendment to the Constitution of Kentucky contained in
22 Section 2 of this Act to be published at least one time in a newspaper of general
23 circulation published in this state, and shall also cause to be published at the same time
24 and in the same manner the fact that the amendment will be submitted to the voters for
25 their acceptance or rejection at the next regular election at which members of the General
26 Assembly are to be voted for. The publication required by this section and KRS 118.415
27 shall be made no later than the first Tuesday in August preceding the election at which
