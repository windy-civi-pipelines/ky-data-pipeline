2 WHEREAS, Kyler Alan Heath was born on January 22, 2003, in Mayfield,
3 Kentucky, to Josh and Ashley Heath; and
4 WHEREAS, from a young age, Kyler had a boundless love for the great outdoors
5 and a passion for hunting and fishing with his father, riding four-wheelers with friends,
6 and playing football; and
7 WHEREAS, Kyler Alan Heath loved working with his father for the family
8 business, where he excelled in his role in the business's construction division; and
9 WHEREAS, Kyler Alan Heath was a friend to many and loved by all. Kyler
10 departed this earthly life far too soon on November 16, 2024. He was preceded in death
11 by his grandfather, Robert Knight, and his uncle Jeffery Bledsoe; and
12 WHEREAS, Kyler Alan Heath will be profoundly missed by his parents, Josh and
13 Ashley Heath and Lyndsey Heath; his siblings, Payton Taylor, Gavin Driscoll, and
14 Cambrey Driscoll; his loving grandparents, Deena "Granny" and Ronnie "Grandad"
15 Heath, and Darren and Amanda Isbell; his uncles, Matt Heath, TJ (Jana) Grooms, and
16 Patrick Grooms; his aunts, Abby (Brandon) Raye, Karlie (Kyle) Balliet, Brooke Grooms,
17 Trista Bledsoe, Jessica Moreland, and Jamie McCuiston; as well as countless family and
18 friends;
19 NOW, THEREFORE,
20 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
21 Kentucky:
22 Section 1. The Senate hereby expresses its most profound sympathy upon the
23 passing of Kyler Alan Heath.
24 Section 2. When the Senate adjourns this day, it does so in honor and loving
25 memory of Kyler Alan Heath.
26 Section 3. The Clerk of the Senate is directed to transmit a copy of this
27 Resolution to Senator Jason Howell for delivery to the family.
