Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS HB 501/EN
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 315 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, when a pharmacist is
6 aware that a practitioner has died, a prescription issued by that practitioner may
7 continue to be dispensed based on the pharmacist's professional judgment and no
8 later than one hundred eighty (180) days following the date on which the
9 practitioner died.
10 (2) Nothing in this section authorizes the dispensing of a prescription that was issued
11 for a controlled substance unless permitted under the federal Controlled
12 Substances Act and regulations of the United States Drug Enforcement
13 Administration.
14 Section 2. 2025 RS HB 495/VO, Section 1, is amended to read as follows:
15 Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary and unless required under federal
16 law, the Department for Medicaid Services and any managed care organization with
17 whom the department contracts for the delivery of Medicaid services are hereby
18 prohibited from expending any Medicaid funds on any of the following:
19 (1) Cross-sex hormones when prescribed or administered primarily or solely for the
20 treatment of gender dysphoria[in amounts greater than would normally be
21 produced endogenously in a healthy person of the same age and sex]; and
22 (2) Gender reassignment surgery to alter or remove physical or anatomical
23 characteristics or features that are typical for and characteristic of a person's
24 biological sex.
