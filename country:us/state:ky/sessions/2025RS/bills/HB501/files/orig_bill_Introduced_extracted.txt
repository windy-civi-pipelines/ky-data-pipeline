Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 1362
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 315 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, when a pharmacist is
6 aware that a practitioner has died, a prescription issued by that practitioner may
7 continue to be dispensed based on the pharmacist's professional judgment and in
8 accordance with the following requirements:
9 (a) A new prescription that has not been previously dispensed may be dispensed
10 no later than ninety (90) days following the date on which the practitioner
11 died; and
12 (b) A prescription that has been previously dispensed and has valid
13 authorization to be refilled may be dispensed no later than one hundred
14 eighty (180) days following the date on which the practitioner died.
15 (2) Nothing in this section authorizes the dispensing of a prescription that was issued
16 for a controlled substance unless permitted under the federal Controlled
17 Substances Act and regulations of the United States Drug Enforcement
18 Administration.
