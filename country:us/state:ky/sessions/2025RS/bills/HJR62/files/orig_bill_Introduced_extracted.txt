12 WHEREAS, on September 30, 1948, Burnside City Marshal George Prentice
13 Southwood was shot and killed at the Burnside bridge while attempting to arrest a local
14 constable for public intoxication; and
15 WHEREAS, the tragic end of these lives has left a void that cannot be filled, and
16 they are mourned by the Burnside community and across the length and breadth of the
17 Commonwealth;
18 NOW, THEREFORE,
19 Be it resolved by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
20 Section 1. The Transportation Cabinet shall designate Kentucky Route 2295
21 from its beginning at United States Route 27 (mile point 0.0) to its intersection with East
22 Lakeshore Drive (mile point 1.1) as a memorial highway and shall, within 30 days of the
23 effective date of this Resolution, erect appropriate signage denoting this designation. The
24 signs erected under this section shall read:
25 "In Memory of Our Fallen Officers
26 Marshals John Coomer, Hiram Gregory,
27 Charlie Wright, & George Prentice Southwood".
