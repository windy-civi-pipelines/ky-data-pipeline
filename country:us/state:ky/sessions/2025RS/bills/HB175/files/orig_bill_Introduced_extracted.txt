Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 1033
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 177 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 Any signage placed on state-maintained highways at the border of another state
6 indicating to motorists that they are entering Kentucky shall not include the names of
7 the Governor or Lieutenant Governor.
8 Section 2. Within 30 days of the effective date of this Act, the Transportation
9 Cabinet shall ensure existing signage on state-maintained highways conforms with the
10 provisions of Section 1 of this Act.
