4 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 12 IS CREATED TO
5 READ AS FOLLOWS:
6 (1) The Office of Safer Communities is hereby established as an independent agency
7 attached to the Office of the Governor, which shall be headed by an executive
8 director appointed by the Governor. The purpose of the office is to develop
9 strategies to coordinate and promote effective ways to reduce community violence
10 through collaborations with local, state, and federal government agencies.
11 (2) As used in this section:
12 (a) "Community violence" means all deaths or injuries caused by a firearm;
13 (b) "Local educational agency" has the same meaning as in 20 U.S.C. sec.
14 7801(30);
15 (c) "Office" means the Office of Safer Communities; and
16 (d) "State educational agency" has the same meaning as in 20 U.S.C. sec.
17 7801(49).
18 (3) The office shall:
19 (a) Obtain alternative funding sources other than the Commonwealth,
20 including gifts, grants, and bequests;
21 (b) Promote or assist in establishing or enhancing community violence
22 intervention and school-based mental health programs;
23 (c) Coordinate with existing community violence intervention and school-based
24 mental health programs to minimize duplication of services;
25 (d) Collect and disseminate data and make recommendations relating to
26 community violence, policy and intervention programs and school-based
27 mental health programs, from both a public safety and a public health
