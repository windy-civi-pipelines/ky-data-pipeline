1 A RESOLUTION recognizing February 6, 2025, as Kentucky Nurses Day in the
2 Commonwealth.
3 WHEREAS, nurses positively impact the health and well-being of Kentuckians
4 across the Commonwealth; and
5 WHEREAS, the nursing profession is recognized as the nation's most trusted
6 profession; and
7 WHEREAS, nurses have been licensed in Kentucky since 1914 and over 100,000
8 nurses are currently licensed in the Commonwealth, working in a variety of settings and
9 specialties; and
10 WHEREAS, the Kentucky Nurses Association, established in 1906, is the only full-
11 service professional organization for the state's entire nurse population; and
12 WHEREAS, Kentucky's nurses have been continuously dedicated to providing care,
13 teaching nursing students, contributing to the scientific understanding of illness and
14 health, and promoting good health policy; and
15 WHEREAS, the Kentucky Nurses Association represents and supports all segments
16 of nursing practice and advocates for nurses, patients, and their families by working with
17 the state legislature and stakeholders;
18 NOW, THEREFORE,
19 Be it resolved by the House of Representatives of the General Assembly of the
20 Commonwealth of Kentucky:
21 Section 1. The members of the House of Representatives hereby proclaim
22 February 6, 2025, as Kentucky Nurses Day in the Commonwealth.
23 Section 2. When the House of Representatives adjourns this day, it does so in
24 honor, recognition, and celebration of Kentucky's nurses and the service and high-quality
25 care that they provide to Kentuckians.
26 Section 3. The Clerk of the House of Representatives is directed to transmit a
27 copy of this Resolution to Representative Mary Lou Marzian for delivery.
