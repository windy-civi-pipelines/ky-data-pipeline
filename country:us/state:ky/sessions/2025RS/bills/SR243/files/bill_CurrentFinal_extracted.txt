3 WHEREAS, Johnnie L. Turner was born on December 24, 1947, in Harlan County,
4 Kentucky, to the late Alonzo and Gladys Hyde Turner; and
5 WHEREAS, Johnnie L. Turner grew up on Pine Mountain, the fourth of 11
6 children, and attended Pine Mountain Settlement School as well as the Red Bird Mission
7 School, where he worked on campus to pay for his books and tuition; and
8 WHEREAS, Johnnie L. Turner forged a lifelong bond and love for the Red Bird
9 Mission School. There, he learned the value of a good work ethic and felt the school was
10 instrumental in shaping him into the person he became; and
11 WHEREAS, Johnnie L. Turner served in the United States Army from 1967 to
12 1969, in the Panama Canal Zone, where he met the love of his life, Maritza, to whom he
13 was married for 55 years; and
14 WHEREAS, after serving his country, Johnnie L. Turner returned to Eastern
15 Kentucky and continued his education at Union College in Barbourville, Kentucky,
16 where he graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in Accounting in 1974. In 1977,
17 he received his Juris Doctor degree from the University of Kentucky College of Law; and
18 WHEREAS, while attending both Union College and the University of Kentucky,
19 Johnnie L. Turner worked at International Harvester in Indiana and often traveled back
20 and forth for work to support his family; and
21 WHEREAS, Johnnie L. Turner was a family man who practiced law in Harlan
22 County for 46 years. He served as the Harlan County School Board attorney for over 20
23 years, and as the City of Cumberland attorney for a number of years; and
24 WHEREAS, Johnnie L. Turner served in the Kentucky House of Representatives,
25 representing District 88 from 1999 to 2002, and also in the Kentucky State Senate,
26 representing District 29 from 2020 to 2024; and
27 WHEREAS, Johnnie L. Turner was an advocate and champion for Eastern
