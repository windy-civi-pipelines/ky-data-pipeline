15 SECTION 2. A NEW SECTION OF KRS 438.305 TO 438.350 IS CREATED
16 TO READ AS FOLLOWS:
17 (1) (a) The Division of Tobacco, Nicotine, and Vapor Product Licensing is hereby
18 created and established within the Department of Alcoholic Beverage
19 Control.
20 (b) The Division of Tobacco, Nicotine, and Vapor Product Licensing shall be
21 managed by a division director, whose appointment shall be subject to KRS
22 12.050.
23 (2) The Division of Tobacco, Nicotine, and Vapor Licensing shall carry out
24 enforcement provisions of KRS 438.305 to 438.350 as they relate to the regulation
25 of alternative nicotine products, tobacco products, and vapor products, including
26 promulgating administrative regulations that govern the sale and distribution of
27 alternative nicotine products, tobacco products, and vapor products.
