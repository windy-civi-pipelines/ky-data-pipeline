2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 350.028 is amended to read as follows:
4 The Energy and Environment Cabinet shall have and exercise the following authority and
5 powers:
6 (1) To adopt administrative regulations after a hearing pertaining to surface coal
7 mining operations including strip mining and the surface effects of underground
8 mining to accomplish the purposes of this chapter;
9 (2) To conduct investigations and hearings under provisions of this chapter or
10 regulations adopted pursuant thereto;
11 (3) To issue, after an opportunity for a hearing, suspension orders or show cause orders
12 requiring an operator, permittee, or person to adopt remedial measures that are
13 necessary to comply with this chapter and administrative regulations adopted
14 pursuant thereto. Failure to attend a hearing shall be excused for good cause shown;
15 (4) To issue, after an opportunity for a hearing, a final order imposing civil penalties
16 for violations of this chapter or directing the Department for Natural Resources to
17 revoke a permit, when the requirements set forth by the notice of noncompliance,
18 order of cessation, or an order of the cabinet requiring remedial measures have not
19 been complied with according to the terms therein. When the secretary or his or her
20 authorized representatives determines that a pattern of violations of any
21 requirements of this chapter or any permit conditions required by this chapter exists
22 or has existed, and if the secretary or his or her authorized representatives also find
23 that the violations are caused by the unwarranted failure of the permittee to comply
24 with any requirements of this chapter or any permit conditions or that the violations
25 are willfully caused by the permittee, the secretary or his or her authorized
26 representative shall forthwith issue an order to the permittee to show cause as to
27 why the permit should not be suspended or revoked and shall provide an
