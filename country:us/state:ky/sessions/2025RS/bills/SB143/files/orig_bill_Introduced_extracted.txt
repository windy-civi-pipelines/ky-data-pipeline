Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 1716
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 116.155 is amended to read as follows:
4 All forms pertaining to registration shall be prescribed and furnished by the State Board
5 of Elections. The registration form shall include the voter's name; date of birth;
6 membership in a political party, political organization, or political group as defined in
7 KRS 118.015, or independent status; Social Security number, if any; mailing and
8 residence addresses, if different; and such other information as the State Board of
9 Elections may deem necessary. No person shall be denied the right to register because of
10 the failure to include his or her Social Security number. All forms which require a voter's
11 signature shall provide for verification by the signer thereof.
