Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 1575
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 157 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section:
6 (a) "Classroom teacher user" means a certified teacher who a school district
7 identifies to the department as being employed primarily to provide
8 instruction to students and who is granted a user account on the online
9 commerce platform to make purchases from the online school supplies
10 vendor;
11 (b) "Department" means the Kentucky Department of Education;
12 (c) "Online" means conducted electronically through the internet; and
13 (d) "Online school supplies vendor" means an online vendor that has been
14 selected by the department to provide school supplies to eligible classroom
15 teacher users under this section.
16 (2) The School Supplies for Kentucky Teachers Program is hereby established to
17 provide eligible classroom teacher users with financial and technical support in
18 purchasing school supplies for use in Kentucky's public schools and subject to
19 availability of funds for that purpose appropriated by the General Assembly.
20 (3) To implement the program, the Kentucky Board of Education shall:
21 (a) Adopt policies and promulgate administrative regulations necessary to:
22 1. Provide for the allocation and management of funds appropriated for
23 the program, the operation of the online commerce platform, and the
24 use by individual classroom teacher users;
25 2. Ensure that purchases made with program funds are restricted to only
26 purchases made by classroom teacher users from the program's online
27 school supplies vendors; and

4 (b) Direct the department to enter into one (1) or more contracts in accordance
5 with KRS Chapter 45A:
6 1. For the development and operation of a user-friendly online
7 commerce platform that:
8 a. Processes the allocation of funds to individual classroom teacher
9 user accounts for the online purchase of school supplies from an
10 online school supplies vendor;
11 b. Includes appropriate fiscal management and payment systems,
12 reporting tools, training tools, and other requirements identified
13 by the board;
14 c. Reports purchases made by a classroom teacher user to a school
15 or district administrator designated by the district; and
16 d. Provides technical assistance to public schools and classroom
17 teacher users on using the platform and identifying allowable
18 purchases from an online school supplies vendor; and
19 2. To select one (1) or more online school supplies vendors for the
20 program, the products that a vendor shall offer, and to establish a
21 price list for the products.
22 Section 2. This Act may be cited as the School Supplies for Kentucky Teachers
23 Act.
