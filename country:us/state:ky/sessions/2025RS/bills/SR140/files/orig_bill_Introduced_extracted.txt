2 Sue "Sandy" Weaver May.
3 WHEREAS, Sandra Sue "Sandy" Weaver May was born on January 3, 1942, in
4 Union, Kentucky, to Sue Bristow and Harold Weaver, as one of six children who were
5 raised to know the value of hard work and a strong family; and
6 WHEREAS, Sandy May graduated from Boone County High School in 1959,
7 where she was active in the Glee Club; and
8 WHEREAS, Sandy May went on to Western Kentucky State College, now Western
9 Kentucky University, where she was a member of Beta Omega Chi and graduated with
10 her bachelor's degree in education in 1964; and
11 WHEREAS, Sandy May later attended Eastern Kentucky University and received
12 her master's degree in 1980; and
13 WHEREAS, while student teaching in Brandenburg, Kentucky, Sandy met and fell
14 in love with Robert "Bob" May. They married in August of 1964 and were married for 54
15 years before his passing in 2019; and
16 WHEREAS, Sandy May and Bob went on to have many adventures as their
17 teaching careers led them to various education positions across the state. They eventually
18 settled in Carrollton, Kentucky, to raise their family; and
19 WHEREAS, beyond her career in education, Sandy May was an elder and president
20 of the congregation at Carrollton Christian Church, a Carrollton City Council member,
21 chair of the Board of Health for the Three Rivers District Health Department, and a
22 faithful member of the Friday Night Supper Club; and
23 WHEREAS, Sandy May retired as the principal of Kathryn Winn Primary School,
24 where she served the Carrollton community and its children for many years. Retirement
25 did not suit her and she continued work as a consultant with the Kentucky Department of
26 Education and mentor for the Kentucky Teacher Internship Program; and
27 WHEREAS, Sandy May was an avid fan of the Kentucky Wildcats. She rarely
