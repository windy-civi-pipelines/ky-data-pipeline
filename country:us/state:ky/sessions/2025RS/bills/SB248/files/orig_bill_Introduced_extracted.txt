Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS BR 1876
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 152.713 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) For purposes of this section, "renewable energy" has the same meaning as in KRS
5 154.20-400.
6 (2) The Center for Renewable Energy Research and Environmental Stewardship is
7 hereby created.
8 (3) The Center for Renewable Energy Research and Environmental Stewardship shall:
9 (a) Provide leadership, research, support, and policy development in renewable
10 energy;
11 (b) Advance the goal of renewable energy;
12 (c) Promote technologies, practices, and programs that increase efficiency in
13 energy utilization in homes, businesses, and public buildings;
14 (d) Emphasize energy policies that would result in cost-conscious, responsible
15 development of Kentucky's energy resources and a commitment to
16 environmental quality;
17 (e) Promote partnerships among the Commonwealth's[state's] postsecondary
18 education institutions, private industry, and nonprofit organizations to actively
19 pursue federal research and development resources that are dedicated to
20 renewable energy;
21 (f) Promote the continued development of public-private partnerships dedicated
22 to promoting energy efficiency through education and outreach;
23 (g) Establish research priorities with approval of the board of directors created in
24 subsection (4) of this section, relating to renewable energy, and develop
25 procedures and processes for awarding research grants to eligible recipients as
26 defined by the board and to the extent that funding is available;
27 (h) Collaborate with the Office of Energy Policy to avoid duplication of efforts,

24 renewable energy who shall be appointed by the Governor;
25 8. One (1) member who shall represent environmental interests to be
26 appointed by the Governor; and

2 (c) The members appointed by the Governor shall serve two (2) year terms and
3 may be reappointed. The members representing specific agencies shall serve

5 (5) The board shall:
6 (a) Adopt operating procedures, including a meeting schedule;
