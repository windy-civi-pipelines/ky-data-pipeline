2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 SECTION 1. A NEW SECTION OF KRS CHAPTER 160 IS CREATED TO
4 READ AS FOLLOWS:
5 (1) As used in this section, "foreign source" means:
6 (a) A foreign government, including an agency of a foreign government;
7 (b) A legal entity, governmental or otherwise, created solely under the laws of
8 one (1) or more foreign states;
9 (c) An individual who is not a citizen or a national of the United States or a
10 trust territory or protectorate thereof; or
11 (d) An agent, including a subsidiary or affiliate of a foreign legal entity, acting
12 on behalf of an individual or entity described in paragraphs (a) to (c) of this
13 subsection.
14 (2) (a) A school district, local board of education, superintendent, or any other
15 agent acting on behalf of a public school shall not accept or receive funds
16 from, or enter into a contract with:
17 1. The government of the People's Republic of China;
18 2. The Chinese Communist Party; or
19 3. Any individual or entity acting directly or indirectly on behalf of a
20 government or party listed in subparagraphs 1. and 2. of this
21 paragraph.
22 (b) A school district, local board of education, superintendent, or any other
23 agent acting on behalf of a public school shall, as a term of a contract
24 entered into by or on behalf of a public school, require the parties to the
25 contract to state that they have no association with the parties listed in
26 paragraph (a)1. to 3. of this subsection.
27 (3) No later than thirty (30) days after receiving funds or entering into a contract

1 with a foreign source, a school district shall submit to the commissioner of
2 education a written report that identifies:
3 (a) The name and country of origin of the foreign source;
4 (b) In a case in which a school receives funds from a foreign source:
5 1. The amount of funds received from that source; and
6 2. Any terms or conditions applicable to the receipt of the funds; and
7 (c) In a case in which a school enters into a contract with a foreign source, the
8 terms and conditions of the contract.
9 (4) By November 1 of each year, the commissioner of education shall file a report
10 with the Legislative Research Commission for referral to the appropriate Interim
11 Joint Committee on Education detailing the reports received by the commissioner
12 of education pursuant to subsection (3) of this section.
