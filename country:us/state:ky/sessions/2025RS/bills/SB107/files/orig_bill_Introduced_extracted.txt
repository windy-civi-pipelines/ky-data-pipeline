8 contrary, a public postsecondary institution, including any affiliated corporation, its
9 officers, employees, and agents, shall be immune from all claims, including class action
10 claims for damages, a declaratory judgment, and equitable relief based on an act or
11 omission if:
12 (a) The claim arises out of or in connection with tuition paid to the public

14 and May 2020;
15 (b) The claim alleges losses or damages arising from an act or omission by the
16 public postsecondary institution during or in response to the COVID-19 emergency;
17 (c) The alleged act or omission of the public postsecondary institution was related
18 to protecting public health and safety interests in response to the COVID-19 emergency
19 in compliance with federal, state, or local guidance, including but not limited to:
20 1. Transition to online or otherwise remote instruction;
21 2. Pause or modification to instruction available through the institution of higher
22 education;
23 3. Closure of, or modification to, operation of on-campus facilities of the public
24 postsecondary institution; or
25 4. The public postsecondary institution offered online and otherwise remote
26 learning options that allowed students to complete the coursework in the academic term
27 that included the months of March, April, and May 2020 and receive academic credit.]
