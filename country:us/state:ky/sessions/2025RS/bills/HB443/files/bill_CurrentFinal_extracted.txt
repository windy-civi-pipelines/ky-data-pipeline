Section 1:
UNOFFICIAL COPY 25 RS HB 443/GA
2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 177.317 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) The Transportation Cabinet shall allow partial control of access on the Hal Rogers
5 Parkway, between the junction with Kentucky Route[KY] 192 and the junction
6 with Kentucky Route[KY] 80, and establish minimum spacing requirements and
7 the manner in which the access is to be provided. Minimum access spacing under
8 this section shall be no less than one thousand two hundred (1,200) feet.
9 (2) The Transportation Cabinet shall include Kentucky Route 80 in the counties of
10 Perry, Knott, and Floyd as part of the Hal Rogers Parkway. The cabinet shall
11 update:
12 (a) Online maps and databases upon the effective date of this Act; and
13 (b) Highway and directional signs upon the normal replacement schedule.
