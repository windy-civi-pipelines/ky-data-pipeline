2 WHEREAS, in the early 1970s, Terry Forcht, attorney and entrepreneur, discovered
3 his aptitude for the business of banking while serving on the board of directors of the
4 Corbin Deposit Bank and Trust; and
5 WHEREAS, in 1985, Terry Forcht and a group of investors opened Tri-County
6 National Bank in Corbin, Kentucky. Over the next several years, Terry Forcht acquired
7 Deposit Bank & Trust, Williamsburg National Bank, Campbellsville National Bank, and
8 several additional banks throughout Kentucky and in Cincinnati, Ohio; and
9 WHEREAS, on December 31, 2007, under Terry Forcht's visionary leadership, the
10 banks merged into one to establish Forcht Bank; and
11 WHEREAS, the core banking principles of Forcht Bank are respecting the
12 customer, offering competitive rates, and serving the community; and
13 WHEREAS, with twenty-three banking centers and three loan production offices,
14 Forcht Bank serves many Kentuckians and Bluegrass businesses; and
15 WHEREAS, in 2025, Forcht Bank will celebrate its 40th Anniversary of exemplary
16 dedication and service to Kentucky communities; and
17 WHEREAS, the 40th Anniversary of Forcht Bank underscores the trust and loyalty
18 of its customers and its commitment to fostering strong, thriving communities;
19 NOW, THEREFORE,
20 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of
21 Kentucky:
22 Section 1. The Senate hereby recognizes and congratulates Forcht Bank on its
23 40th Anniversary and honors it for forty years of growth and success.
24 Section 2. When the Senate adjourns this day, it does so in honor and
25 celebration of Forcht Bank.
26 Section 3. The Clerk of the Senate is directed to transmit a copy of this
27 Resolution to Senate President Robert Stivers for delivery.
