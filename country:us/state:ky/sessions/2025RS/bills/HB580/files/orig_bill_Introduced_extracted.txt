2 Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the Commonwealth of Kentucky:
3 Section 1. KRS 309.0836 is amended to read as follows:
4 (1) An applicant for registration as a temporary alcohol and drug peer support specialist
5 shall pay the board an initial fee for registration, and shall:
6 (a)[(1)] Be at least eighteen (18) years of age;
7 (b)[(2)] Have obtained a high school diploma or equivalent;
8 (c)[(3)] Have completed at least forty (40) classroom hours of board-approved
9 curriculum;
10 (d)[(4)] Have passed a written examination that has been approved by the board;
11 (e)[(5)] Have signed an agreement to abide by the standards of practice and code
12 of ethics approved by the board;
13 (f)[(6)] Attest to being in recovery for a minimum of twelve (12) consecutive
14 months immediately preceding the date of registration from a substance-
15 related disorder;
16 (g)[(7)] Have completed at least:
17 1.[(a)] Sixteen (16) hours of ethics training;
18 2.[(b)] Three (3) hours of domestic violence training;
19 3.[(c)] Two (2) hours of training in the transmission, control, treatment,
20 and prevention of the human immunodeficiency virus;
21 4.[(d)] Ten (10) hours of advocacy training;
22 5.[(e)] Ten (10) hours of training in mentoring and education; and
23 6.[(f)] Ten (10) hours of training in recovery support;
24 (h)[(8)] Have submitted two (2) letters of reference from certified alcohol and
25 drug counselors or licensed clinical alcohol and drug counselors;
26 (i)[(9)] Live or work a majority of the time in Kentucky; and
27 (j)[(10)] Have complied with the requirements for the training program in suicide

25 drug peer support specialist;

27 be provided by a registered alcohol and drug peer support specialist;
